DIGITAL PRODUCTION PRINTING

Print Services has a variety of digital printing equipment to handle whatever
needs you may have. We offer digital Black and White Printing, Color Printing,
Business Card Printing, Letterhead Printing, and Envelope Printing on our range
of digital presses.

For B&W and Color printing, use our online Job Ticketing
[https://printcenter.cod.edu] ordering system.

Business Cards, Envelopes, and Letterhead submission forms are located in the
Quick Links on the right.




JOB TICKETING

Job Ticketing is our newly designed, user friendly online job submission tool.
Job Ticketing is fully integrated with the PaperCut system used for Smart Print
Fleet Printing. There is no need to create an additional account, simply log in
with your COD credentials to begin submitting orders. If you don’t see what
you’re looking for on the product list, don’t hesitate to reach out to Print
Services at (630) 942-2299. Print Services staff will assist you on the proper
way to submit a request for whatever product you’re looking for.

LARGE FORMAT PRINTING/SIGNAGE/ENGRAVING

Print Services utilizes a Flatbed UV Ink Printer with a roll media attachment to
provide a wide range of finished products. Some of our current services include:
Vinyl Banners, Posters, Window Clings, Floor Graphics, Wall Clings, Custom
Stickers, Window Film, Canvas Printing, Formboard Printing, Yard Signs
(Coroplast), and Vinyl Lettering. Our services are constantly growing based on
the needs of our customers. Reach out with your ideas, and Print Services staff
can help bring them to life!

Our accompanying flatbed cutter allows us to contour cut virtually any desired
shape. Instructions for designing files for cutting can be found on the Large
Format Printing/Signage/Engraving
[/about/campus-departments/campus-services/print-services/sign.aspx] Page.

Print Services also handles the creation of Desk Plates, Employee Name Tags, and
Student Worker Name Tags.

All submission forms are located in the Quick Links on the right.
