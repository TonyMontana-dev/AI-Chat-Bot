OPEN HOUSE

College of DuPage's Fall Open House is an informative and welcoming event
designed to offer prospective students, their families, and the community a
firsthand look at all the college has to offer. Come and discover why COD is the
ideal place to begin your college journey while also getting a head start on the
enrollment process.

Saturday, Nov. 11
10 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Physical Education Center (PEC), Arena



Register for Open House
[https://admissions.cod.edu/RecruitNewWFE/Events/EventDetails?eventId=538b6ca6-e050-ee11-a91b-005056b7f0fe]



At Open House, you'll have the chance to:

 * Meet with an Admissions and Outreach team member to find out how easy it is
   to start your degree or certificate at COD.
 * Talk to our expert faculty from various disciplines and learn which programs
   are the right fit for you as you start your educational journey.
 * Learn how to have a successful college experience by using student services
   offered at no additional cost.
 * Explore clubs and organizations you can join to gain leadership experience.


AGENDA

 * 9:30 a.m.: Check-in begins
 * 10 a.m. to 1 p.m.: Table Visits
 * 11 a.m. to noon: Information Session (PEC 133)
 * 11 a.m. to noon: Information Session in Spanish (PEC 131)


TABLE VISITS

Interact with over 60 program representatives and faculty members to discover a
variety of programs, establish meaningful connections, and potentially identify
a path toward your future career. Furthermore, you'll have access to student
support resources, such as Registration Services, Financial Aid, Counseling,
Academic Advising and Transfer Services, Career Guidance, Records, and Student
Life, offering valuable insights on starting at COD and guiding you through the
enrollment process.


CHECK-IN INFORMATION

Check-in will take place in the Physical Education Center (PEC), Arena. For
directions, visit our Maps & Directions
[/about/visitor-information/maps-directions/index.aspx] page.
