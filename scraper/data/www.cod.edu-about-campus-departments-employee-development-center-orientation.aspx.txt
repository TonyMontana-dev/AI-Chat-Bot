EMPLOYEE WALKING TOURS (IN-PERSON)

Who: All Employees
When: Once per month (see Cornerstone > Learning > Events Calendar)
Length: 90-minutes (conducted in-person)
Registration: Cornerstone
Topics: This is in-person walking tour will help employees become familiar with
navigating buildings throughout main campus. 


COD MANAGEMENT ORIENTATION (LIVE, VIRTUAL)

Who: All New Full-Time non-teaching Employees
When: Once per term
Length: Varies
Registration: By invitation
Topics:  Learn about HR topics that managers need (eg. Paid time off, Leaves,
Employee Relations, etc.).  Hear from other leaders about what it means to be a
manager at COD from both transactional and transformational perspectives.
 Discover on-demand resources that will help you now and in the future as you
navigate management and leadership at College of DuPage. 


NEW EMPLOYEE ORIENTATION (LIVE, VIRTUAL)

Who: All New Full-Time non-teaching Employees
When: Every other month 
Length: 4 hours
Registration: By invitation 
Topics:  Learn about what it means to be an employee at COD and how you can help
support and promote the Mission, Vision, Values and Strategic Long Range Plan of
the College.  Meet presenters from various departments (including HR, Benefits,
COD Police, Environmental Health & Safety, Information Technology, Library,
Title IX office). 


ONBOARDING SESSIONS (LIVE, VIRTUAL)

Who: All Employees 
When: Once per month (see Cornerstone > Learning > Events Calendar)
Length: 90-minutes (conducted via Zoom) 
Registration: Cornerstone
Topics: This session covers a thorough tour of the inside.cod.edu employee
portal.  


WELCOME - PORTAL TOUR FOR NEW FULL-TIME FACULTY (LIVE, VIRTUAL)

Who: New Full-Time Faculty
When: Fall Term
Length: Varies
Registration: Will be notified by EDC/Academic Affairs
Topics: As a complement to the New Faculty Institute, this session includes a
tour of the inside.cod.edu employee portal with additional information about
benefits for Faculty.  


STUDENT WORKER ORIENTATION (ON-DEMAND)

Who: Student Employees
When: On-demand
Length: Two hours
Registration: Cornerstone (Managers/Supervisors can register their Student
Employees)
Topics: Federal Drug-Free Schools and Campus Regulations, Student Employee
Orientation Packet, Student Employee Orientation video


NEW EMPLOYEE ORIENTATION: SELF-PACED CURRICULUM (ON-DEMAND)

Who: All part-time Employees 
When: On-demand
Length: Two hours
Registration: Cornerstone
Topics: As you navigate this on-demand content in Cornerstone, you will see a
Welcome video, Campus emergency response information, Risk Management, Virtual
Campus Tour, Maps and Directions, and the College Policy Manual.
