Scholarships can extend the life of military education benefits and ease the
burden of college costs. College of DuPage offers over 120 scholarships.
Veterans are highly encouraged to apply for Veteran specific and program
specific scholarships. 


ILLINOIS ASSOCIATION FOR COLLEGE ADMISSIONS COUNSELING TRANSFER SCHOLARSHIP

Scholarships for students transferring from an Illinois community college to an
IACAC member 4-year institution as new students.


SCHOLARSHIP ELIGIBILITY

 * Have completed a minimum of 36 semester hours at an Illinois community
   college
 * Have a minimum 3.0 cumulative GPA from all institutions attended
 * Be an applicant to an IACAC member four-year institution
 * Submit the IACAC Transfer Scholarship Application
   [https://www.iacac.org/transfer-scholarship/application/]


SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

 * Complete the IACAC Transfer Scholarship Application, including essay
 * Submit unofficial transcripts from all institutions attended
 * Recipient will be required to submit official transcripts
 * Recipient must enroll at an IACAC member postsecondary institution as a new
   student


COLLEGE OF DUPAGE SCHOLARSHIP APPLICATION DEADLINES

 * Fall Term: Jan. 15 to March 31 
 * Spring Term: Sept. 1 to Nov. 1
