ENGLISH PROFICIENCY

International students who apply for admission to College of DuPage are required
to submit TOEFL iBT or IELTS Academic test scores as part of the admission
process. A minimum TOEFL score of 40 or an IELTS score of 3 is required for
admission to the College. 

 * Students with a TOEFL iBT score of 79 or higher or an IELTS Academic score of
   6.5 or higher will be considered college-ready and will be eligible to enroll
   in academic courses.
 * Students with a TOEFL iBT score of 40-78 or an IELTS Academic score of 3-6
   will complete all relevant placement exams and will be placed in English 1101
   or Academic ESL courses per the current placement guidelines.
 * Students with a TOEFL iBT score of 39 or lower or an IELTS Academic score of
   2.5 or lower will not be eligible for admission to College of DuPage. 


EXCEPTIONS

The following may qualify for an exemption to the TOEFL and IELTS requirement:

 * International students from countries** where English is the primary/native
   language.
 * International students who have taken ACT with a composite score of 20 or
   higher.
 * International students who have graduated from a U.S. high school.
 * International students who have successfully completed English 1101 or
   equivalent at an accredited U.S. college or university.
 * International students who have earned a bachelor's or master's degree from
   an accredited U.S. college or university.
 * International students currently residing in the United States and enrolled
   at language training schools are required to demonstrate language proficiency
   by completing the TOEFL iBT, IELTS Academic test, ACT or College of DuPage
   Kaplan International Tools for English placement test. The test is divided
   into two parts. Students are placed based on the cumulative score from Part 1
   (Multiple Choice) or the average score from Part 2 (Writing and Speaking).

A review of the online sample materials is recommended in order to determine the
minimum proficiency required for Level 1 ESL courses
[/academics/els/index.aspx].

**Countries include: Australia, the Bahamas, Barbados, Belize, Bermuda, British
Virgin Islands, Canada (except Quebec), Cayman Islands, Ireland, Jamaica, New
Zealand, Singapore, South Africa, Trinidad and Tobago, United Kingdom, and U.S.
Virgin Islands.
