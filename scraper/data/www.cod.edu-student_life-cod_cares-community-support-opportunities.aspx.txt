VOLUNTEERS NEEDED

 * Help stuff backpacks full of back-to-school essentials for clients of
   Abundant Children and Family Services Agency [https://abundantcfsa.org/].  
 * Volunteer [https://volunteer.dupagepads.org/agency/index/] weekly in the
   resource center or provide front desk help at DuPage Pads Interim Housing
   Center [https://dupagepads.org/].
 * Families and friends can enjoy the sunshine as they help map
   existing pollinator habitats with the DuPage Monarch Project
   [https://dupagemonarchs.com/]. Training on the iNaturalist app will be
   included. Volunteer at dupagemonarchs@gmail.com [dupagemonarchs@gmail.com]. 
     
 * Sew neck coolers to be included in care packages shipped overseas to deployed
   service members through Operation Support Our Troops-America
   [https://www.osotamerica.org/].  Contact Traci
   [events@osotamerica.org?subject=Neck%20Cooler%20Sewing%20Instructions] for
   simple instructions.  
 * People's Resource Center
   [https://docs.google.com/document/d/11ES0pE1HIR2PUleuRiuLWs1P8cptu-frYRYVJ4oC7MU/edit]
   is looking for volunteer assistance in their food pantry and tutoring
   center.   

 * Visit the Giving DuPage Volunteer Search Portal for current and urgent
   volunteer needs.

Please note: COD Cares compensated service hours are only applicable to group
service activities hosted by COD Cares and, therefore, are not applicable to the
above list.


IN-KIND DONATIONS

 * Shop online for essential items
   [https://360youthservices.org/housing/donate-supplies/] needed in 360 Youth
   Services [https://360youthservices.org/] emergency shelter and transitional
   housing.
 * Take advantage of BOGO deals and share school supplies
   [https://abundantcfsa.org/donations] with young clients of Abundant Children
   and Family Services Agency [https://abundantcfsa.org/].  

 * Special hair care products are needed for clients of Bridge Communities
   [https://www.bridgecommunities.org/index.html].  
 * The Outreach House [https://www.theoutreachhouse.org/] supports the community
   through food pantry, clothes closet and job assistance services. In-kind
   donations [https://www.theoutreachhouse.org/inkind] of food, clothing and
   infant necessities are urgently needed.  

 * DuPage Pads [https://dupagepads.org/] has a critical shortage of these
   essentials
   [https://dupagepads.org/wp/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/Donation-Needs-Final.pdf].
   Drop off donations [https://dupagepads.org/solutions/needed-items/] at the
   Client Services Center. 


NEED ASSISTANCE?

For a more comprehensive list of additional local area resources, visit
211dupage.gov [https://211dupage.gov/] or call 211.


COD CARES ABOUT WHAT YOU CARE ABOUT

Email CODCares@cod.edu [codcares@cod.edu] to tell us about your favorite
national or local organization and why it is special to you! We can include it
on this page or add it to our growing list of monthly group service activities.
