Our Mission: To provide quality services in an efficient and effective manner
directly or indirectly to students, faculty, staff and the community. Our
services will be provided with integrity, honesty, respect and responsibility in
support of the academic and service activities of the college. Our daily
interactions with others will reflect these shared ethical values as we fulfill
our obligations to our clients in a spirit of service and teamwork.


IMPORTANT INFORMATION

 * Campus Services Department provides amenities for NON-COD related projects to
   College of DuPage employees. Call ext. 3500 or 2299 for quote. College of
   DuPage related work takes priority over personal job requests.
 * Campus Services no longer acceptd cash or checks as a form of payment. All
   payments to Campus Services will need to be processed via credit or debit
   card.
