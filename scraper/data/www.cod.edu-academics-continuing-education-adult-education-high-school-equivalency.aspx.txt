Take the first step toward your educational goals and improve your earning
potential by working toward your high school equivalency (HSE). COD's free HSE
program prepares you to take and pass the GED® or other high school equivalency
exam. Don't let the past stop your future; get your GED® and keep going!

This program is geared toward adults age 16 and over who: 

 * Lack a high school diploma
 * Have good reading skills
 * Need to prepare to earn a high school equivalency certificate

Note:  A letter of withdrawal from high school must be provided by students who
are 16 or 17 years old.


BENEFITS OF HIGH SCHOOL EQUIVALENCY

 * Classes and textbooks are free
 * Free tutoring available
 * Easy registration
 * Supportive teachers
 * Several locations available near you
 * Classes available in Spanish
 * Tests can be taken in English or Spanish
 * Online classes are available


WHAT YOU'LL LEARN

You'll receive instruction in the five areas covered on the tests:

 * Social studies
 * Science
 * Interpreting literature and the arts
 * Mathematics
 * Writing skills

Instruction is also offered on the U.S. and Illinois Constitutions. A test
covering these areas must be passed if it was not passed in high school.

Integrated Career and Academic Preparation System (ICAPS)
[/academics/continuing-education/adult-education/icaps.aspx] 
Learn how to attain your high school equivalency while earning in-demand
certification in information technology or welding through ICAPS at College of
DuPage.


GET STARTED TODAY

All students will be required to take a placement test prior to starting their
high school equivalency classes. Walk-in testing is available in Glen Ellyn,
Addison, Carol Stream, Naperville and Westmont. Contact (630) 942-3697 for more
information on testing hours and location.
