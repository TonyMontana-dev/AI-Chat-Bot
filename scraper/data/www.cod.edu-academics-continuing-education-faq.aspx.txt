 * Registration [/academics/continuing-education/registration.aspx]

Continuing Education offer courses and workshops at the College of DuPage campus
in Glen Ellyn. Classes are also offered at off-campus locations including COD
Centers in Addison, Carol Stream, Naperville and Westmont. Learn more about
Continuing Education below.

How do I register?

Register and pay for courses online through myACCESS [http://myaccess.cod.edu/].
Be sure to select "Continuing Education Classes" in the menu. You may also call
Continuing Education at (630) 942-2208 with your course details for easy
registration.

How do I pay for my classes?

For all questions relating to payment or refunds, read: "Paying for Continuing
Education Classes [/academics/continuing-education/ce_financial_info.aspx]."

Who can register for non-credit classes?
 * Youth Academy: Students in grades K through 12  
 * Adult Enrichment, Professional Development and Credit Classes: 18 years and
   older  
 * Lifelong Learning Institute: Open to any adult, but these courses are
   specifically designed for 50 years and older. Reduced senior fees are
   available for those 55 years and older.
   
   

When should I register?

At least 10 days prior to the start of the class.

What about cancellations?

Please help prevent canceled classes by enrolling early. Classes will be
canceled prior to the start date if enrollment is too low.

Are out-of-district students charged a higher fee for Continuing Education
classes?

In-district residents and out-of-district students are welcome to take our
classes for the same registration fee.
