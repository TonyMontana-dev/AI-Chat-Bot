CAROL STREAM CENTER

College of DuPage's Carol Stream Center offers many of the courses that are
available at COD's Glen Ellyn campus. The center provides large and comfortable
classrooms, a state-of-the-art computer lab, convenient parking, and is home to
one of the college's Learning Commons where students can receive a variety of
academic support services, including testing. Contact the Carol Stream Learning
Commons and Testing Center for additional services and testing hours
[/academics/testing/hours.aspx].


Services Offered at Carol Stream Center
 * Registration Services
 * Payments (check or credit card)
 * Counseling, Advising and Transfer Services (by appointment)
 * Assistance using college resources, myACCESS and Blackboard
 * SAT/ACT Test Score Submission
 * ELA Placement Testing
 * Open computer lab, for students and community members
 * Electronic access to COD Library resources
 * Anatomy & Physiology Open Lab (call for hours)
 * Math Assistance: Wednesday, 5 to 8 p.m.
 * Voter Registration
 * Notary Services

Carol Stream Learning Commons and Testing Center

The Carol Stream Learning Commons (CSLC), close to home and conveniently located
within the Carol Stream Center, 500 Kuhn Road, offers placement and academic
testing for students, as well as specialized testing for the community. Learning
Commons and Testing Center staff also help COD students discover the many free
academic support services available to them. 


TESTING SERVICES

 * All Testing Centers are open on a walk-in basis for placement and academic
   testing. No appointment is needed. 
 * Hours and locations [/academics/testing/locations.aspx] for all Testing
   centers.
 * Tests offered:
   * Placement testing
   * College of DuPage academic course testing
   * TABE (placement test for GED preparation course)
   * GED Tests and specialized tests for certifications or licensing
   * Carol Stream classroom makeup testing
   * A complete list of tests offered
     [/academics/testing/tests-administered/index.aspx] at College of DuPage
 * Private testing room available. - Call to reserve. 


ACADEMIC SUPPORT

 * Computers and printing for students and community members
 * Wi-Fi available 
 * College library website assistance
 * Math Assistance: Wednesday, 5 to 8 p.m.
 * Online Placement Test Preparation Resources
   [/academics/learning_commons/resources/placement-test-review.aspx]

Visit the Learning Commons [/academics/learning_commons/index.aspx] for
additional information about in-person academic assistance options including
Math Assistance [/academics/learning_commons/mathematics-assistance/index.aspx],
Tutoring Services [/academics/learning_commons/tutoring/index.aspx] and Writing,
Reading, Speech Assistance [/academics/learning_commons/writing/index.aspx].
