College of DuPage has eight academic divisions committed to helping students
succeed. Each division contains a variety of programs that offer both degrees
and certificates.

woman taking a photo at a COD art show
[/_resources/img/top-level/artscommunicationshospitality-twocolumncard.jpg]


ARTS, COMMUNICATION, AND HOSPITALITY

Arts, Communication and Hospitality provides students an opportunity to create,
perform, study and participate in the arts.

Learn More [/academics/divisions/art-comm-hospitality/index.aspx]
business technology image
[/_resources/img/top-level/businesstechnology-twocolumncard.jpg]


BUSINESS AND APPLIED TECHNOLOGY

Business and Applied Technology prepares students with skills needed for
immediate success in the job market.

Learn More [/academics/divisions/business-technology/index.aspx]

[/_resources/img/top-level/continuingedpublicservice-twocolumncard.jpg]


CONTINUING EDUCATION AND PUBLIC SERVICES

Continuing Education provides learning experiences for you to connect with
members of the community.

Learn More [/academics/continuing-education/index.aspx]

LEARNING RESOURCES

Learning Resources staff can provide support services through the Library,
Learning Commons and the Testing Center.

Learn More [/academics/divisions/learning-resources/index.aspx]
liberal arts teacher [/_resources/img/top-level/liberalarts-twocolumncard.jpg]


LIBERAL ARTS

Liberal Arts is comprised of English, English Language Services, History,
Humanities, Interpreting, Languages including American Sign Language, Philosophy
and Religious Studies.

Learn More [/academics/divisions/liberal-arts/index.aspx]

[/_resources/img/top-level/nursingandhealthscience-twocardcolumn.jpg]


NURSING AND HEALTH SCIENCES

More than 30 health-related certificate and degree areas of study make up
Nursing and Health Sciences.

 Learn More [/academics/divisions/health-sciences/index.aspx]

PUBLIC SERVICES

College of DuPage offers future and current first responders with quality
training in state of the art facilities.

Learn More [/academics/continuing-education/public-services/index.aspx]

SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY, ENGINEERING AND MATH DIVISION

STEM focuses on integrating science, technology, engineering and math in
