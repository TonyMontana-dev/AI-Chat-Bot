LARGE FORMAT PRINTING/SIGNAGE

Print Services utilizes a Flatbed UV Ink Printer with a roll media attachment to
provide a wide range of finished products. Some of our current services include:

 * Vinyl Banners
 * Posters
 * Window Clings
 * Floor Graphics
 * Wall Clings, Custom Stickers
 * Window Film, Canvas Printing
 * Formboard Printing
 * Yard Signs (Coroplast)
 * Vinyl Lettering
 * Wide Format Scanning up to 36 inches

Our services are constantly growing based on the needs of our customers. Reach
out with your ideas, and Print Services staff can help bring them to life!

TURNAROUND TIMES

Turnaround times vary depending on the size and details of the submitted job.
Contact Print Services at x2299 to get an estimated turnaround time on any Large
Format Printing/Signage/Engraving orders.




ORDERING FORM & INSTRUCTIONS

 * Large Format Printing & Engraving Order Form  
   [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/pdf/sign_engrave_work_order.pdf]

Note: All PDF Forms must be saved to your computer before filling out and
submitting. Completing these forms in a web browser will not submit the forms
correctly.

Fill out each section of the Order Form as it applies to the work you are
requesting. See sample form below for reference:

 1. Fill out all required fields except “Work Order #.” Account number must be
    provided and will end with the 5909015 object code.
 2. All ‘Wide Format Printing – Signs, Banners, Posters’ will be printed
    directly onto the selected media.
 3. Submitted files should be designed at the desired size. Scaling of smaller
    files can result is resolution issues. Files should be saved in one of the
    following file types: PDF, PNG or JPEG.
 4. Special Instructions – Use this section for any details that are needed but
    not included on the standard form. Additional instructions can be includes
    in the email submitted with the order form and file.
 5. All large format print jobs will need to be picked up. Small items
    (stickers, desk plates, and name tags) can be delivered. Select your mail
    stop from the drop down list at the bottom of the form.
 6. Click ‘Submit’ when your form is filled out. This will open an email in
    outlook with the form attached. Please attach the files that are to be
    printed along with this email before sending.
