COSTS, SCHOLARSHIPS & AID

Figuring out how to pay for college can be difficult. Choosing College of DuPage
gives you the opportunity to get a great education that will save you thousands
of dollars.

The Office of Student Financial Assistance provides a comprehensive range of
services that bridge the gap between the financial resources of students and
their families and the cost of education at COD, reducing the economic barrier
to obtaining an education.

Learn about financial aid options, apply for scholarships or determine the cost
of attending COD.
