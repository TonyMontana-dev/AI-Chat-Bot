KYOTO AND OSAKA, JAPAN STUDY ABROAD

Immerse yourself in the language and culture of Japan. Held in cooperation with
Kyoto International Academy in Kyoto (KIA), classroom instruction comes alive
with the surroundings serving as a hands-on classroom and language laboratory.
Students practice and improve knowledge of the language by interacting with the
Japanese people on the street, on buses and in museums, theaters, shops,
restaurants and cafes, as well as with their host family.


2024 STUDY ABROAD PROGRAM INFORMATION

 * Program Dates: June 11 to July 12, 2024
 * Fee: $5,374, includes round trip airfare from Chicago, transportation in
   Japan, on-site Japanese classes, accommodations, some meals and some
   excursions. Tuition (6-7 credits) is extra.
 * For more information, contact Shingo Satsutani [satsutan@cod.edu?subject=] or
   fssa@cod.edu [fssa@cod.edu?subject=Costa%20Rica].

Passport Information: If you do not have a passport, or your passport expires
within six months of the program dates, you MUST apply for a passport or renew
your passport immediately. Passports can take up to four months to obtain. Do
not wait until you are accepted into the program.
