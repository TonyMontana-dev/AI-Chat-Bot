The COD News Bureau crafts dynamic and compelling stories to support the
College's mission and vision through media outreach, social media strategy and
multimedia. The media relations arm of the Marketing and Communications division
[/about/campus-departments/marketing-communications/index.aspx], the News Bureau
is responsible for developing press releases and obtaining media placements in
local, regional and national media outlets, enhancing visibility of the
College's news, programs, students, faculty, events and thought leadership.
