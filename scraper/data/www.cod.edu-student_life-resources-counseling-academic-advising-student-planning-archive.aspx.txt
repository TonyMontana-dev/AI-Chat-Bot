Students who have enrolled at College of DuPage prior to Fall 2005 and have not
broken enrollment can elect to meet degree requirements current at the time they
began their studies. Copies of the Student Planning Worksheets for degrees
ranging from 1998 through 2023 are accessible below. Students can request
official evaluations of degree progress through the Office of Student Records.
