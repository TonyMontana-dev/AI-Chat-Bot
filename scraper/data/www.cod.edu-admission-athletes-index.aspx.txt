START THE ADMISSIONS PROCESS

Admission Requirements

Meet with an Admissions Representative/College of DuPage Coach
To meet with an Admissions Representative, visit cod.edu/contact_admissions
[/admission/admissions_contact/index.aspx]. To learn more about the varsity
athletic offerings and coach contact information, visit gochapsgo.com
[https://gochapsgo.com].

Create a Profile and Submit the General Admissions Application
Create your profile and complete your application by visiting admissions.cod.edu
[http://admissions.cod.edu]. New applicants are required to submit the $20
application fee. Students qualifying for an application fee waiver should visit
the Admissions Policies page [/admission/admissions_policies.aspx] before
applying.

Once you submit your application, we will create your COD Username and send it
to your personal email. An email titled "COD Student Portal Username" will
contain your COD Username and information on setting up your password and
accessing your student email account.

Apply for Financial Assistance or Scholarships/ Explore Payment Plans
Visit the FAFSA [https://studentaid.gov/] website and add school code 006656 to
determine your financial aid eligibility. Scholarships for athletic
participation at the college are not currently offered.

Learn more about applying for financial assistance
[/costs/financial_aid/index.aspx] and view available scholarship opportunities
[/costs/scholarships/index.aspx].

Athletic Eligibility Requirements

Complete Athletics Eligibility Requirements
To complete requirements to be eligible to compete as a COD athlete, see
information on additional steps below:

 * Athletic Eligibility Forms Packet - Contact your head coach or pick-up packet
   at Athletics Main Office (PEC 121). All eligibility forms from this packet
   must be completed and hard copies submitted to Head Coach
 * Athletic Medical Forms Packet - Contact your head coach or pick-up packet at
   Athletics Main Office (PEC 121). All medical forms from this packet must be
   completed and hard copies submitted to Athletic Trainers (PEC 108).

Submit Official Transcripts and Test Scores
Includes ALL official high school (with graduation date) and college
transcripts, ACT, SAT, AP or CLEP Scores. If submitting prior to graduation or
the end of the semester, you will need to submit your transcript in progress,
and your final transcript for NJCAA eligibility.

See Transcript Submission guidelines/dates for your specific sport in your
Athletic Eligibility packet.

Enrollment Requirements

Complete Placement Tests (if applicable)
Complete the reading, writing, ELS/ESL and math placement tests. Testing
assistance is available in our Learning Commons
[/academics/learning_commons/index.aspx].

Attend New Student Advising & Registration (NSAR) Session for Athletes
To schedule your appointment with Counseling & Advising
[/student_life/resources/counseling/index.aspx], call (630) 942-2259. When
scheduling, indicate that you are an athlete, as there may be separate advising
sessions available.

Meet with the Office of Access and Accommodations (if applicable)
If you need accommodations for a disability, our Center for Access and
Accommodations [/student_life/resources/access-accommodations/index.aspx] can
help you with your needs.

Register for Classes
To register, login to your InsideCOD Portal [http://inside.cod.edu] account. You
must be registered as a full time student (12+ credit hours) to be eligible to
play in your respective sport.

Pay for Classes or Setup a Payment Plan
To pay for classes or setup a payment plan, login to your InsideCOD Portal and
review the "Student Payment Information" section. All students should explore
payment plan options.

Purchase Text Books/Course Supplies
Books and other materials you need for your classes can be purchased at the
Bookstore on campus or online at codbooks.com [http://www.codbooks.com]

Attend New Student Orientation (NSO)
At NSO [https://myaccess.cod.edu], you'll be able to meet other Chaparrals, get
more familiar with campus and hear everything COD has to offer.
