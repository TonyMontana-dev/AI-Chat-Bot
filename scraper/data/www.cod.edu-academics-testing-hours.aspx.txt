SPECIAL NOTES

 * Math TABE tests will not be issued 2 hours before closing.
 * Reading TABE tests will not be issued 3 hours before closing.
 * ALEKS Math and Kaplan International Tools for English Placements tests will
   not be issued 90 minutes before closing.
 * All other tests will not be issued one hour before closing.
 * Exams collected ten minutes prior to closing.
 * Specialized testing is by appointment
