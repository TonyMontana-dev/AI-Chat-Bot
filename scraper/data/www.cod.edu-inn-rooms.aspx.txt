IN-ROOM AMENITIES

 * Floor to ceiling windows
 * Complimentary wireless internet throughout
 * 55” flat screen TV
 * Telephone
 * On-desk outlets for computer and other electronics
 * Alarm clock
 * Feather pillows available in-room
 * In-room safe
 * Iron and ironing board
 * Walk-in marble shower
 * Hair dryer
 * Daily housekeeping services (Due to COVID-19 restrictions
   [/inn/covid-info.aspx], this service is currently unavailable.)
 * Wake-up call available upon request
