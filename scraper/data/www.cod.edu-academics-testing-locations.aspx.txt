The Testing Center provides testing services for students, faculty and community
members. Testing Centers are located on the College of DuPage Glen Ellyn Main
Campus and at all of the COD Centers. 

Testing Center hours vary by location [/academics/testing/hours.aspx]. Most
exams [/academics/testing/tests-administered/index.aspx] are available at all
locations. Some exams require an appointment. Contact the specific Testing
Center for more information.

eligible students to take proctored tests remotely for certain courses.
