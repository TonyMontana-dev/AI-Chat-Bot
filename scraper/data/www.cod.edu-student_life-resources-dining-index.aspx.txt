DINING AT COD

Home [https://www.cod.edu/]Student Life
[https://www.cod.edu/student_life/]Resources
[https://www.cod.edu/student_life/resources/]Dining at COD

Sodexo [/student_life/resources/dining/images/sodexo.jpg]Sodexo's College of
DuPage Dining Services provides an exciting and innovative dining services
program for the entire College of DuPage community. You'll find great food,
friendly people and a comfortable atmosphere in which to enjoy it all. You have
a variety of food concepts to choose from, all conveniently located on campus
and within walking distance of classrooms and labs.

Vending machines are located campus-wide for your convenience and are accessible
24 hours a day. The Dining Services department is dedicated to providing you
good quality food at reasonable prices. For those with specific dietary needs,
contact Dining Services at (630) 942-2666.


FEATURED AREAS


BREW 425

BREW 425 [/student_life/resources/dining/images/brew-425-logo.jpg]Brew 425 will
serve Starbuck's high-quality coffees. Our premium beverage options include rich
espressos, lattes, Teavana Teas, frappuccinos, and refreshers. We will also
serves a variety of pastries and grab and go items. Conveniently located on
second floor of Student Services Center (SSC)
[/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/bic_ssc.aspx].

 


CHAPS

Featuring rotating weekly menus. 

 


SIMPLY TO GO

Simply to Go [/student_life/resources/dining/images/simply-to-go.jpg]Convenient
grab and go salads, sandwiches, and snacks made fresh daily. Conveniently
located in the E.E. Gibson Cafeteria on the first floor of the Student Resources
Center (SRC) [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/src.aspx]. 

 


SUBWAY

Subway [/student_life/resources/dining/images/subway.jpg]America’s number one
choice for sub sandwiches, Subway offers delicious subs on fresh-baked bread,
custom-made and topped with the highest-quality ingredients. Conveniently
located in the E.E. Gibson Cafeteria on the first floor of the Student Resources
Center (SRC) [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/src.aspx]. 

 


SOGO CASH CARD

SOGO Cash Card [/student_life/resources/dining/images/sogo-cash-card.jpg]The
SoGo Cash Card is a personal spending and gift card. It's fast and easy to pay
for your purchases at participating locations. No need to carry a credit card or
cash and there are no fees! See the cashier to get yours today or manage your
account online [https://sogocashcard.prepaidaccess.com/consumer].
