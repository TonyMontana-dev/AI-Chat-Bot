 * Transfer Scholarship Opportunities
   [/academics/transfer/transfer-scholarship-opportunities.aspx]

A number of scholarship opportunities are available exclusively for students
transferring from College of DuPage to another college or university. Each
transfer scholarship has a different set of criteria. Some transfer scholarships
are based on financial need while others are based on achievement, location or
demographics.


HONORS TRANSFER SCHOLARSHIPS

College of DuPage has partnered with Aurora University, Elmhurst University,
Millikin University and North Central College to offer scholarships to COD
Honors students [/academics/academic_opportunities/honors/scholarships.aspx].
Awards vary from $1,500 to full tuition. 


ILLINOIS OPPORTUNITIES

 1. Golden Apple Scholars Program -
    [https://www.goldenapple.org/scholars#scholars/apply] A teacher preparation
    scholarship for Illinois college transfer students who have the
    determination and drive to be highly effective teachers in Illinois.
    Scholarship, preparation and mentorship provided.
 2. Private Illinois Colleges and Universities (PICU)
    [https://picuonline.org/scholarships/]- Professional college network
    scholarship; deadline in June; awards range from $500 to $1,000.
 3. Illinois Association for College Admission Counselors (IACAC) Transfer
    Scholarship [https://www.iacac.org/transfer-scholarship/]- Five awards of
    $1000 are available; fall deadline is in March and spring deadline is in
    December.
 4. Illinois Association of Collegiate Registrars and Admission Officers
    (IACRAO) Transfer Scholarship
    [https://www.iacrao.org/iacrao-scholarships-information]- $1000 scholarships
    are available for eligible students who apply. 


NATIONAL OPPORTUNITIES

 1. Jack Kent Cooke Undergraduate Transfer Scholarship -
    [https://www.jkcf.org/our-scholarships/undergraduate-transfer-scholarship/how-to-apply/]Application
    opens in October and closes in January; up to $55,000 annually to attend any
    four-year accredited undergraduate school and study any discipline.

 2. Phi Theta Kappa (PTK) Honor Society Scholarships
    [https://www.ptk.org/scholarships/]- Requires a PTK membership; amounts and
    deadlines vary.
 3. US National Scholarship
    [https://www.thedream.us/scholarships/national-scholarship/guidelines/]-
    Scholarship available for Undocumented/DACA students; deadline in February;
    students are required to attend a partner college.


ADDITIONAL SCHOLARSHIP TIPS

 1. Always file your Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA)
    [https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa] when you apply for transfer
    admission.
 2. College of DuPage offers guaranteed admission agreements
    [/academics/transfer/guaranteed-transfer-admission.aspx] with colleges and
    universities that offer scholarship opportunities ranging from $1000 to full
    tuition. 
 3. Apply for competitive scholarships offered at your transfer college or
    university. Ask your admission counselor what else you can apply to once
    accepted to your destination.
 4. Merit scholarships are automatically awarded at the time of your admission
    application. Stackable Phi Theta Kappa (PTK) Honor Society scholarships are
    offered at private institutions for PTK members.
 5. Completing the CSS profile [https://cssprofile.collegeboard.org/] for
    colleges that will accept or require a CSS profile is another way to
    maximize scholarship dollars.
