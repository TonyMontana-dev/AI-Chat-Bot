COLLEGE OF DUPAGE BEST IN-STATE OF ILLINOIS EMPLOYERS

College of DuPage has been named among Forbes America's 2021 Best In-State
Employers List. [/news/2021/september/forbesbestinstate.aspx]

The Office of Human Resources at College of DuPage is dedicated to the College's
mission of excellence in teaching and learning and student success through
providing quality service and a supportive environment to all employees and
departments of the College. Browse the links below to learn more about COD's job
and career opportunities, training and development services, employee resources
and College policies.

College of DuPage Human Resources is committed to excellence.


WHY COD?

College of DuPage provides an excellent workplace for its employees:

 * Team-based organization in a collaborative environment.
 * Widely diverse workforce of more than 3,000 employees.
 * Flexible, creative work environment with vast employee growth and
   development.
 * Competitive salaries with generous benefit options.
 * Cutting-edge, high-tech environment.


ANNUAL CAMPUS SECURITY REPORT

A copy of the Annual Campus Security Report
[/about/police_department/statistics.aspx] is available upon request. The report
includes statistics about crimes that occurred on campus and information related
to institutional policies concerning campus security, sexual assault, and other
matters. A copy of this report can be obtained by contacting the College of
DuPage Police Department at (630) 942-2000 or by visiting the COD Police
Department [/about/police_department/statistics.aspx].
