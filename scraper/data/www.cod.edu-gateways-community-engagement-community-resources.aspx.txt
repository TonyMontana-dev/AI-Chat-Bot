 * Service to the Community
   [/gateways/community-engagement/service-to-the-community.aspx]

College of DuPage is an invaluable resource to the residents of DuPage County
and beyond. In addition to its excellent faculty and staff who provide students
with the highest quality of academic instruction and support, the College offers
a myriad of programs, services, arts and cultural initiatives for individuals
seeking unique and engaging experiences. COD also provides a pathway for local
businesses and nonprofits pursuing development opportunities.

We invite you learn more about the ways College of DuPage supports the residents
and organizations within Community College District 502 through robust
programming that encourages entrepreneurship, personal and business growth, and
exploration.
