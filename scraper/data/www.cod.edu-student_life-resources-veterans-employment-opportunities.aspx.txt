INTERNSHIPS

 * VetTech Internship - Every summer, Fermilab in Batavia, IL hires student
   veterans for a 10-week, full-time paid internship as a technician in their
   laboratory. Fermilab seeks COD student veterans in majors like engineering,
   CIS, CIT, automotive service technology, manufacturing, HVACR and other
   fields to fill these slots. If you are interested in participating, consider
   applying on Fermilab's website [http://diversity.fnal.gov/vettech/].


PROFESSIONAL CAREER MENTORING

American Corporate Partners (ACP)
[https://www.acp-usa.org/mentoring-program/program-overview] is a free,
non-profit program that assists America's veterans with job and career
preparation. ACP Mentors are ready to help you build your résumé, prepare for
interviews, discuss career options and succeed in your job search.

 * A one-to-one year-long mentorship with a corporate professional in the career
   field of your interest.  ACP mentors
   [http://www.acp-usa.org/about-us/partners] come from more than sixty
   companies including 21st Century Fox, Bloomberg, Deloitte, GE Intel, Johnson
   & Johnson, Lockhead Martin, Raytheon, PwC, USAA and many others.  
 * Note that you were referred by College of DuPage on your application. If you
   have questions, email info@acp-usa.org [info@acp-usa.org?subject=] or call
   (212) 752-0700.


JOB OPPORTUNITIES FOR VETERANS

Veterans are sought for the following employment opportunities.

 * The College of DuPage Veterans Services office
   [/student_life/resources/veterans/index.aspx] actively seeks student veterans
   to fill roles as receptionists and mentors to assist other student veterans
   pursuing their education. View student job opportunities
   [https://cod.csod.com/ux/ats/careersite/5/home?c=cod].
 * Special Opportunities [https://www.smithfieldfoods.com/careers]
 * Operation Support Our Troops - America
   [http://www.osotamerica.org/get-involved/itemsneeded/], OSOT based in Lisle
   is always in need of care items and volunteers to pack care packages for U.S.
   troops overseas.  
 * YMCA Urban Warriors
   [https://www.ymcachicago.org/programs/youth-safety-and-violence-prevention-urban-warriors],
   A Veteran to youth mentoring opportunity. The YMCA of Metro Chicago's Youth
   Safety and Violence Prevention initiative pairs combat experienced Veterans
   with youth exposed to violence in some of Chicago's most dangerous
   neighborhoods.
 * Honor Rewards
   [https://www.dupagecounty.gov/elected_officials/recorder/honor_rewards.php] -
   Offered by the DuPage County Recorder's Office, Honor Rewards is an ID card
   that helps Veterans receive discounts, promotions and other incentives from
   participating local businesses. Veterans can sign up for the Honor Rewards ID
   Card at the Recorders Office or online.
