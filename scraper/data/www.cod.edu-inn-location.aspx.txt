LOCATION

The Inn at Water’s Edge resides in the The Culinary & Hospitality Center (CHC)
at College of DuPage which is located off Fawell Boulevard in Glen Ellyn,
Illinois. The building includes parking for the Inn at Water’s Edge customers.
View map and directions [/inn/pdf/location.pdf] 

maps/directions [/inn/images/maps_bottom.jpg]/inn/pdf/location.pdf

Call (630) 942-6888 to reserve a room.
Nightly room rates include complimentary fitness center use during stay.
