CONTINUING EDUCATION

Expand your skills, further your career and discover interests that enhance your
life. Continuing Education can provide the perfect learning experience for you
and connect you with other talented members of the College of DuPage community.

Our courses and workshops are offered on campus in Glen Ellyn and at a variety
of off-campus locations including COD Centers in Addison, Carol Stream,
Naperville and Westmont. We also offer programs at several high schools and
local businesses.

You do not need to apply to College of DuPage to register for Continuing
Education classes. You can self-register online
[https://myaccess.cod.edu/WebAdvisor/WebAdvisor?TOKENIDX=1108278801&type=M&constituency=WBCE&pid=CORE-WBCE] or
call our Registration staff at (630) 942-2208 for personalized assistance.
