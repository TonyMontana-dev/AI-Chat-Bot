Select an evaluation company from the list of National Association of Credential
Evaluation Services [https://www.naces.org/members](NACES)
[https://www.naces.org/members] members to perform your international transcript
evaluation. Each company may have different requirements for your document
submission and will have different fees for their services. 


INTERNATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL TRANSCRIPTS

Select a course-by-course evaluation of your high school transcript. General or
basic evaluation reports are typically acceptable for high school (secondary
school) documents. If you took chemistry in high school and plan to take
chemistry at College of DuPage, you should select a course-by-course evaluation.


INTERNATIONAL COLLEGE/UNIVERSITY TRANSCRIPTS

Select a course-by-course or detailed evaluation report of your college or
university transcript.


TRANSCRIPT EVALUATION COMPANY SUBMISSIONS

Official evaluation reports should be sent directly to College of DuPage from
the company or can be brought to the Office of Student Records in the original,
unopened envelope.

If you have any questions about which type of evaluation you should choose,
contact the Office of Student Records at evaluation@cod.edu
[evaluation@cod.edu]or (630) 942-3829.
