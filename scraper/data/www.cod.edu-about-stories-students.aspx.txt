STUDENT STORIES

College of DuPage students and alumni have gone on to become leaders in their
fields of expertise. They have become entrepreneurs, medical professionals,
renowned artists, civic leaders, educators and service providers.

Hear how COD has made a difference in the lives of students and alumni by
viewing our student success stories. Search by name or major or check out a
select group of featured students and alumni. 

If you’re ready to get started achieving your career goals, apply for admission
[/admission/index.aspx].
