STUDENT REGISTRATION FORMS

The following registration and appeal forms are available to current,
prospective and transfer students. If you have any questions about registering
for classes or completing a form, contact Student Registration Services at (630)
942-2377 or registration@cod.edu [registration@cod.edu].

 * Audit Enrollment Change Request
   [/registration/forms/audit-enrollment-change.aspx]
 * Cooperative Agreement Request
   [/registration/forms/cooperative-agreement.aspx]
 * Late Withdrawal Appeal [/registration/forms/late-withdrawl-appeal.aspx]
 * Medical Appeal [/registration/forms/medical-appeal.aspx]
 * Permit to Register [/registration/forms/permit-to-register.aspx]
 * Senior Citizen Tuition Waiver for 2023-2024
   [/registration/pdf/senior_tuition_waiver.pdf]
 * Third Party Billing Application [/registration/pdf/third_party_app.pdf]
 * Tuition Appeal [/registration/forms/tuition-appeal.aspx]


CONTACT INFORMATION

Office of Student Registration Services
Student Services Center (SSC), Room 2221
Phone: (630) 942-2377
Email: registration@cod.edu [registration@cod.edu]

Regular Hours

 * Monday through Thursday, 8 a.m. to 7 p.m.
 * Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Student Help Desk: (630) 942-2999

Individuals who need language assistance, call Campus Central at (630) 942-3000
or email campuscentral@cod.edu [campuscentral@cod.edu].
