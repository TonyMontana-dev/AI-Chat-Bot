INN AT WATER'S EDGE

The Inn at Water’s Edge boasts elegant, well-appointed guestrooms with walk-in
showers, flat screen TVs and complimentary wireless internet access. Each room
offers floor-to-ceiling windows facing a peaceful lake. With a fireplace in the
lobby, a breakfast room and a front desk staffed 24 hours a day, the Inn has
everything one expects to find at a boutique hotel. This amazing LEED Certified
facility complements the adjacent McAninch Arts Center, making it possible for
area residents and out-of-town visitors to enjoy a show with an overnight stay.
The student focus makes this boutique hotel truly a unique experience. Although
run by a professional team; students also play a role in the guest experience
through internships and student worker opportunities.
