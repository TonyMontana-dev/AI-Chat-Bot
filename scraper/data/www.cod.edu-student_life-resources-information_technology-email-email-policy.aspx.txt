College of DuPage sends all official communications to students through their
official COD email (dupage.edu). Students have the option to forward these
communications to a personal email account. Check out your COD email to get the
message about:

 * Official COD communications
 * Financial aid communications
 * Petition to graduate
 * Transfer credit evaluation
 * Prerequisite proof 
 * Sensitive FERPA-related communications
 * Transcript ordering
 * Important messages from instructors

 


FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is my College of DuPage email?

Your email address is your myaccessid@dupage.edu that was created after you
applied to College of DuPage. If you forgot your email address, go
to inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu/students] and click on Retrieve/Forgot
Username. Follow the instructions provided at the top of the input form. You
will need to know the email address you provided when you applied for admission
to the college. If you have questions about this email address, contact the
Student Help Desk:


INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY SERVICES STUDENT HELP DESK

(630) 942-2999
studenthelp@dupage.edu [studenthelp@dupage.edu?subject=website%20inquiry]

How do I set or retrieve my password?

Go to inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu/students] and click on Set/Forgot
Password. Follow the instructions provided at the top of the input form.

How do I change my password?

Go to inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu/students] and click on Change
Password. Follow the instructions provided at the top of the input form.

How do I set up email on my mobile device?

Visit the Guide to Student Email

How do I forward my COD email to my personal email account?
