 * Fiscal Year Grant Awards
   [/about/campus-departments/grants/fiscal-grant-awards.aspx]

In FY2023, College of DuPage managed over $12 million in grant awards that
supported academic programs, student scholarships, arts, public radio
programming, facilities, equipment, and community services. For more
information, contact Marcia Frank at (630) 942-4611 or frankm182@cod.edu
[frankm182@cod.edu].


FISCAL YEAR 2023

 * ComEd/Openlands, ComEd Green Region Grant
   Anna Bakker
   Funding to plant native pollinator plugs on two areas of the main campus.
 * Consortium of Academic and Research Libraries in Illinois (CARLI), Support
   for Creation of Open Educational Resources (SCOERs)
   Lauren Kosrow
   Support for a summer faculty accelerator retreat to author a new OER for a
   Physics course and remix OER for two Addictions Counseling courses.
 * Corporation for Public Broadcasting, Community Service Grant
   Dan Bindert
   General community service grant to provide support for the WDCB radio
   station.
 * FMA and the Nuts, Bolts, & Thingamajigs Foundation, Dream It, Build It Camp
   James Tumavich & Brenda Large
   Support for a summer camp for middle school students with hands-on activities
   in Advanced Manufacturing.
 * FMA and the Nuts, Bolts, & Thingamajigs Foundation, GADgET (Girls Adventuring
   in Design, Engineering & Technology) Camp
   James Tumavich & Brenda Large
   Support for a summer camp for girls in grades 6 through 8 with hands-on
   activities in Advanced Manufacturing.
 * Gene Haas Foundation, Gene Haas Foundation Scholarships
   James Tumavich
   Funding to support students enrolled in CNC Technologies programs.
 * Illinois Arts Council Agency, Partners In Excellence
   Diana Martinez & Janey Sarther
   General operating support for programs at the MAC.
 * Illinois Arts Council Agency, Public Radio & TV Basic and Operating Grants
   Dan Bindert
   General support for programs at WDCB Radio.
 * Illinois Board of Higher Education, Illinois Cooperative Work Study (ICWS)
   Krystina LaSorsa
   Cooperative work-study program for approximately 12 students in partnership
   with at least seven employers.
 * Illinois Community College Board, FY23 Adult Education & Literacy Program
   Dan Deasy
   Federal and State allocated funding to support district ABE/GED/ESL
   programming.
 * Illinois Community College Board, FY23 College Bridge Program
   Diana Del Rosario
   Funding to support a direct intervention program aimed at helping
   underrepresented high school students complete a postsecondary credential.
 * Illinois Community College Board, FY23 PATH
   Jared Deane
   Program funding to support students studying Registered Nursing, CNA,
   Respiratory Care Therapist, and EMT Technician/Paramedic/Attendant through
   scholarships, books, supplies, software, and a dedicated Success Coach.
 * Illinois Community College Board, FY23 Mental Health Early Action on Campus
   Act Funding
   Steven Gustis
   Allocated state funding intended to address gaps in mental health services on
   college campuses across Illinois.
 * Illinois Community College Board, Perkins Postsecondary Career & Technical
   Education (CTE) Program
   Jonita Ellis
   Federal and State allocations to support the development of academic
   knowledge in addition to technical and employability skills of CTE students
   in accordance with the FY23 Program Plan.
 * Illinois Community College Board, Early Childhood Access Consortium for
   Equity (ECACE) Program
   Jean Zaar
   Federal allocation over a 3-year grant period to provide streamlined pathways
   to degrees, licenses, and credentials to members of the early childhood
   incumbent workforce.
 * Illinois Community College Faculty Association, Dr. Joseph T. Cipfl Workshop
   Karin Evans
   Support to develop and host an English Composition workshop.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, Youth Apprenticeship
   Expansion - Project Hire-Ed
   Danielle Kuglin-Seago
   Funding to support an expansion of the pre-apprenticeship program into
   regional high schools eventually leading to apprenticeship programs in
   collaboration with DuPage Regional Office of Education and GPS-ED.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, FY23 Procurement
   Technical Assistance Center (PTAC)
   Jean Lin
   Federal funding to support COD Business Development Center activities to meet
   government contracting business development needs of small businesses
   throughout District 502 and the northern Illinois/western collar county
   regions.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, FY23 Small Business
   Development Center (SBDC)
   Ute Westphal
   State and Federal funding to the Illinois SBDC to provide one-stop business
   management assistance to individuals and small businesses.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, Tourism Attractions &
   Festivals Grant
   Diana Martinez, Janey Sarther, & Justin Witte
   Support for the Andy Warhol Portfolios: A Life in Pop exhibition at the Cleve
   Carney Museum of Art.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, General
   Infrastructure Grant I
   Don Inman
   Capital funding for the construction of the Immersion Classroom on campus.
 * Illinois Department of Commerce & Economic Opportunity, General
   Infrastructure Grant II
   Don Inman
   Capital funding for the construction of the Digital Fabrication Lab on
   campus.
 * Illinois Department of Human Services, Certified Recovery Support Specialists
   (CRSS) Success Program
   Jason Florin & Andrea Polites
   Funding to support the development and implementation of a CRSS certification
   program.
 * Illinois Green Economy Network (IGEN), EV Charging Station Funding
   Don Inman & Chris Wosachlo
   Subaward funding from the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency for
   installing an electric vehicle charging station on campus. 
 * Illinois Green Economy Network (IGEN), General Membership Funding
   Don Inman & Chris Wosachlo
   Subaward funding from the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency to install
   LED grow lights in the Greenhouse.
 * Illinois Green Economy Network (IGEN), IGEN Project Funding
   Don Inman & Chris Wosachlo
   Subaward funding from the Illinois Environmental Protection Agency to install
   LED lights in the TEC Manufacturing and Welding Labs.
 * Institute of International Education, IIE American Passport Project
   Sue Kerby
   Funding to assist Pell Grant recipients in obtaining a U.S. Passport and
   facilitate international experiences as part of their postsecondary
   education.
 * Internal Revenue Service, Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA)
   Beverly Carlson & Maureen McBeth
   Funding to support the VITA program at COD, hosted by the Accounting
   Department. The program helps community members receive free tax filing
   preparation services.
 * National Security Agency, GenCyber Student Program
   Tony Chen & Justin Wagner
   Supports a summer camp and year-round activities for middle school and high
   school teachers focusing on cybersecurity curriculum development and career
   exploration.
 * National Security Agency, GenCyber Teacher Program
   Tony Chen & Justin Wagner
   Supports a summer camp and year-round activities for middle school and high
   school students focusing on cybersecurity skills development and career
   exploration.
 * National Science Foundation, LSAMP-PUMA STEM Program
   Cory DiCarlo
   Five-year grant supporting seven four-year universities and College of DuPage
   to promote STEM research opportunities for underrepresented groups.
 * National Science Foundation, Scholarships for STEM (S-STEM)
   Tom Carter & Richard Jarman
   Funding for scholarships and other training opportunities for students
   pursuing STEM majors.
 * National Science Foundation, NOYCE PRIDE Program
   April Zawlocki
   Subaward in collaboration with Elmhurst University to recruit local high
   school students into a STEM teacher preparation program.
 * National Junior College Athletic Association (NJCAA) Foundation, Sport
   Opportunity Grant
   Ryan Kaiser
   Funding to start a competitive Men’s Lacrosse team.
 * National Junior College Athletic Association (NJCAA) Foundation, Sport
   Opportunity Grant
   Ryan Kaiser
   Funding to start a competitive Women’s Lacrosse team.
 * Northwestern Medical System, Community Benefit Grant Program
   Danielle Kuglin-Seago & Jared Deane
   Support to pilot a Medical Assistant Pathways and Apprenticeship program with
   Northwestern Medicine Health Systems.
 * U.S. Department of Education, Title III
   Federal funding to help serve low-income students by strengthening academic
   quality, institutional management, and fiscal stability.
 * U.S. Department of Health and Human Services/Health Resources and Services
   Administration, Illinois House District 6 Community Project Funding
   Lisa Stock & Jared Deane
   Federal appropriations funding to purchase nursing program training
   equipment.
 * U.S. Department of Justice, Patrick Leahy Bulletproof Vest Partnership
   Joseph Mullin & Jim Nehls
   Support to offset costs related to purchasing bulletproof vests for College
   of DuPage Police Officers.
 * U.S. Department of Justice, Body-Worn Camera Policy and Implementation
   Program
   Joseph Mullin & Jim Nehls
   Federal funding to support the procurement and deployment of body-worn
   cameras for COD Police Officers.
 * U.S. Department of State/Northern Virginia Community College, FY23 Community
   College Initiative Program (CCIP) Student Exchange
   Kathleen Smid
   Provides opportunities for international students to develop leadership and
   professional skills, as well as English language proficiency, while studying
   at a community college in the United States.
 * U.S. Department of State/World Learning, Increase and Diversify Education
   Abroad for Students (IDEAS)
   Susan Kerby & Julia DiLiberti
   Supports the College of DuPage Africa Initiative, which integrates service
   learning into a sustainable model for interdisciplinary study abroad while
   highlighting non-traditional study abroad disciplines. This grant features a
   partnership between COD and EDU Africa.
