If you need to select classes, but counseling appointments are full, there are a
variety of advising tools and resources that can help you get the answers you
need while you wait for your appointment. 


CREATING YOUR CLASS SCHEDULE

The Steps to Creating an Educational Plan
[/student_life/resources/counseling/pdf/steps_to_creating_an_educational_plan.pdf] tool
can help you select your classes and develop your educational plan. Once you've
completed the plan, meet with a counselor or advisor to confirm your goals.


ACADEMIC RESOURCES

Explore academic programs of study and courses at College of DuPage.

 * Areas of Study [/academics/programs/index.aspx] - COD offers programs from
   several Academic and Career Pathways, including Business and Applied
   Technology, Culinary and Hospitality, Education, Fine and Applied Arts,
   Health Sciences, Public Services, STEM and more.
 * Career and Technical Program Information
   [/academics/programs/career_technical_info_sheets.aspx] - Career and
   Technical programs teach students technical, job-specific skills to be
   college and career ready.
 * Course Catalog [https://www.cod.edu/catalog/current/index.aspx] - The Course
   Catalog provides an overview of programs, classes, services and course
   requirements.


EDUCATIONAL PLANNING RESOURCES

Plan your academic schedule by proofing your prerequisites, completing a Student
Planning Worksheet
[/student_life/resources/counseling/academic-advising/student-planning.aspx] and
running a degree audit. 

 * How To Complete a Student Planning Worksheet Video
   [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CL7oO_0LDDA] - Learn how to complete a
   Student Planning Worksheet so you can choose the correct general education
   core curriculum classes that are required for the type of degree you are
   pursuing. 
 * Proofing Prerequisites [/records/proof-of-prerequisite.aspx] - Verify you
   have met course prerequisites so you can enroll in the classes you need to
   take to continue your education. 


TRANSFER RESOURCES

Gather information on transferring to another college, online schools and
graduate programs.

 * College Board [http://www.collegeboard.com/]- College Board can help you with
   college search and planning.
 * Illinois Transfer [http://www.itransfer.org/] - The Illinois Transfer portal
   can assist you with transferring your course credit to other Illinois
   institutions. 
 * Peterson's [http://www.petersons.com/] - Peterson's provides information on
   education, test preparation and career planning.
 * Princeton Review [http://princetonreview.com/] - Princeton Review offers
   online test preparation for the ACT and SAT.
 * Transferology [https://www.transferology.com/state/il?all] - Transferology
   enables you to enter your college courses and find equivalent courses at
   other schools.
