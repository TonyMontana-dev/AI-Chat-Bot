CONTACT INFORMATION

For more information, fill out the contact form
[/admission/admissions_contact/contact-admissions.aspx].

The Admissions Office is operating during regular business hours:

 * Monday and Tuesday, 8 a.m. to 7 p.m.
 * Wednesday through Friday, 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Phone: (630) 942-2626
Email: admissions@cod.edu [admissions@cod.edu] 
Chat: Chat Online [/chat.aspx]

Walk-in appointments are available Monday through Thursday from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m.
on a first-come, first-served basis for a 15-minute meeting. Students who need
more time can schedule a virtual or in-person appointment with their admission
representative [/admission/admissions_contact/index.aspx].
