In these month-long language and cultural immersion programs led by COD faculty,
spend your summer exploring new places as you improve your language skills and
earn 6-7 COD credits.


COSTA RICA

Explore beautiful Costa Rica in a full cultural immersion experience, with
intensive Spanish language study. Earn six to seven credits (depending upon
placement) in Spanish language and culture. Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/costarica/index.aspx]


FRANCE

Experience French joie de vivre while living in Bordeaux. Improve your language
skills and enjoy cultural immersion. Earn six to seven credits (depending upon
placement) in French language and culture. Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/france/index.aspx]


GERMANY

Old meets new is the theme highlighting your daily experience while studying
German in the cosmopolitan city of Munich. Earn six to seven credits (depending
on placement) in German language and culture. Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/germany/index.aspx]


ITALY

Experience the warm hospitality of the Italian people and enjoy the breathtaking
scenery of the Tuscany region. Earn six to seven credits (depending on
placement) in Italian language and culture. Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/italy/index.aspx]


JAPAN

Live and study in the ancient Japanese capital of Kyoto, totally immersed in
Japanese culture and intensive Japanese language study. Earn six to seven
credits (depending on placement) in Japanese language and culture. Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/japan/index.aspx]


SPAIN

Immerse yourself in the Spanish language and culture as you explore magnificent
castles and royal palaces in Madrid. Enjoy centuries of artistic treasures. Earn
six to seven credits (depending on placement) in Spanish language and culture.
Learn More
[/academics/academic_opportunities/field/studyabroad/spain/index.aspx]


PASSPORT INFORMATION

If you do not have a passport, or your passport expires within six months of the
program dates, you MUST apply for a passport or renew your passport immediately.
Passports can take up to four months to obtain. Do not wait until you are
accepted into the program.
