COD EXPERTS GUIDE

Home [https://www.cod.edu/]College of DuPage Newsroom
[https://www.cod.edu/news/]COD Experts

Drawing on their extensive research and knowledge, College of DuPage faculty
members and staff are experts in their field and can provide insight into an
issue or trend. For assistance with locating an expert, contact the COD News
Bureau at newsbureau@cod.edu [newsbureau@cod.edu]. COD faculty and staff can
join the guide by creating an expert profile
[https://airtable.com/shrfHE9y0MY0rNtK0]. 

Looking for a speaker? Through the Speaker's Bureau, COD Experts are available
to engage with local organizations through in-person and virtual lectures,
performances and discussions, providing a glimpse into the College's wide array
of knowledge. Fill out the Speakers Bureau Request Form
[https://cod.edu/about/campus-departments/marketing-communications/speaker-request-form.aspx]
to request a speaker for an upcoming program or event.

Search for an expert by name, topic or keyword below.
