HSTI COMMUNITY WORKSHOPS

HSTI Community Workshops are free for viewers to explore topics on crime
prevention, frauds and scams, emergency preparedness and more. 


MENTAL HEALTH BASICS AND SUICIDE PREVENTION



This HSTI Community Workshop is to provide basic information about common mental
disorders and the impact of these disorders on overall functioning. Participants
will learn about the signs and symptoms associated with various types of mental
disorders and how to respond to individuals in need of help. Participants are
also taught how to distinguish mental health crisis situations and identify
suicide risk behaviors, which may constitute a medical emergency. Information
about mental health treatment options and available therapies, including local
resources are provided.

PAST COMMUNITY WORKSHOP

The Opioid Epidemic - A Call to Action

This webinar occurred on Dec. 3, 2020. The following video of the webinar is
available for viewing.



Watch DuPage County Coroner, Dr. Richard Jorgensen, provide statistics of the
rising deaths in our county due to opioid overdoses and counterfeit medications.

Hear a compelling personal story from a couple about their struggle with opioid
addiction, and their subsequent success with treatment and recovery.

Learn about the Narcan Program, HOPEDUPAGE, and treatment and recovery options.
Discover what resources are available and where to find them.
