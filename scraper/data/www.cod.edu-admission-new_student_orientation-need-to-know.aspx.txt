New Student Orientation gives new Chaparrals the chance to interact with other
students, discover the services and resources available on campus, and form
connections that continue into the school year.

Please check back for updated Spring 2024 New Student Orientation information.

What happens at NSO?

Please refer to the NSO Agenda [/admission/new_student_orientation/agenda.aspx]
and General Information page
[/admission/new_student_orientation/general-info.aspx] for event details.
Advising and registration do not take place at NSO. 

How can I sign up for NSO?

Spring 2024 NSO sign-ups will be available online. Please check back for updated
information.

Was Fall 2023 NSO in-person?
Yes, Fall 2023 NSO was held in-person on the College of DuPage main campus.
Those who missed the event can access a recording here.
[https://www.youtube.com/live/7aYE8GHu_ww?feature=share]
What do I need to bring?

For quick and easy check-in, bring your student ID or ID number.

Is there a fee to attend NSO?

There is no fee to attend NSO.

Can I attend NSO if I’m not registered for classes?

We encourage you to register for classes prior to attending, but it is not
required. PLEASE NOTE: Advising and registration do not take place at NSO.

What is the E-NSO book?

The E-NSO book is where you can find the NSO agenda and information about campus
departments and resources.

Read More [https://online.flipbuilder.com/cod-mcs/pmqz/]

I still have questions. Who do I contact if I need assistance?

For additional assistance, please contact Student Life at (630) 942-2243 or
studentlife@cod.edu [studentlife@cod.edu ?subject=VNSO%20Questions].

For Americans with Disabilities Act accommodations, call (630) 942-2141 (voice)
or (630) 858-9692 (TDD).
