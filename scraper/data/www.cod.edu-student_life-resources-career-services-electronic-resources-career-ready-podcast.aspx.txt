Join Career Services every other Tuesday and listen to the Career Ready Podcast
to learn about resumes, cover letters, LinkedIn, interviewing and all the things
you need to be career ready.

Hosted by Career Services Specialists Rebecca Harrington, Michelle Malik and
Pierre Michiels, listeners can submit career questions to careerpodcast@cod.edu
[Careerpodcast@cod.edu] for a chance to have them answered on a future show.
