Jean Lin [/business-development-center/images/staff/jean_lin.jpg]

Jean Lin


JEAN LIN

Center Program Manager
(630) 942-3041
Email: Lini@cod.edu [Lini@code.edu]

Jean first started at the Center in September of 2017 as the Illinois SBDC
International Trade Center (ITC) Program Manager. She has been involved in
exporting since her early 20s' with business experience in multiple markets. Her
background includes export strategic planning, market development, product
development and global supply chain management. She is a Certified Global
Business Professional (CGBP) by the National Association of Small Business
International Trade Educators (NASBITE International). 

Ileen Kelly [/business-development-center/images/staff/kelly.jpg]

Ileen Kelly


ILEEN KELLY

Program Manager, Illinois APEX Accelerator Program
(630) 942-2184
Email: kellyi421@cod.edu [kellyi421@cod.edu] 

Ileen Kelly came on board July 2019 as the Illinois PTAC Program Manager. She
brings to the table over 10 years of providing Leadership and Capacity Building
training for both small businesses and non-profit organizations. She spent over
10 years in the workforce development arena across multiple industries assessing
and providing training for both incumbent and future workforce. She also spent
over 25 years in marketing, advertising and public relations – Managing the
public message for national clients and brands. Her primary responsibility is to
advise clients to grow their business through local, state and federal
government contracting which includes certifications.

David Lezondra [/business-development-center/images/staff/david-lezondra.jpg]

David Lezondra


DAVID LEZONDRA

Program Coordinator, Illinois Small Business Development Center
(630) 942-3031
Email: lezondrad@cod.edu [lezondrad@cod.edu?subject=]

David joined the Illinois SBDC team in Oct. 2022 as the Program Coordinator. His
career and expertise in all things “small business” began with Bank of America
where he was a top-ranked Small Business Consultant, banker, and advisor for
over 15 years. He then parlayed to a career in Workforce Development, working as
a Business Services Representative and Job Developer for WorkNet DuPage. David
created strategic alliances and partnerships with local businesses and employers
while promoting no-cost, state-funded, and needs-based resources, and grant
programs for businesses. David has bachelor’s degrees in Business Administration
majoring in Business Management, and Visual Communications focusing on Graphic
Design and Arts.

Colleen Sall [/business-development-center/images/staff/colleen-sall.jpg]

Colleen Sall


COLLEEN SALL

Program Coordinator, Illinois APEX Accelerator
(630) 942-2184
sallc@cod.edu [sallc@cod.edu]

Colleen Sall joined the team as APEX Accelerators Coordinator in January 2023.
Colleen has over five years of experience with College of DuPage, serving the
local community within the Provost’s Office and Continuing Education. She also
worked with the business incubator/accelerator Innovation DuPage, helping to
support the collaboration and development of entrepreneur businesses with major
corporate and leading educational institution partners. She has a background of
more than 15 years in corporate marketing communications and product management
with industry-leading and Fortune 500 companies. She has written and edited
industry articles, newsletters and press releases, and has overseen the creative
work of external public relations firms and advertising agencies. Additionally,
she managed trade show and user group meetings and was an event product
specialist speaker. Her primary responsibility is to advise clients to grow
their business through local, state, and federal government contracting which
includes certifications.

Ute Westphal [/business-development-center/images/staff/westphal.jpg]

Ute Westphal


UTE WESTPHAL

Program Manager, Illinois Small Business Development Center
(630) 942-2630
Email: westphalu@cod.edu [westphalu@cod.edu]

Ute Westphal came on board in May 2017 as the Illinois SBDC Program Specialist.
She has been an entrepreneur in two different countries and brings in a solid
understanding of today's world of entrepreneurs. In addition, she has been
coaching and consulting with small business owners for 4+ years. This position
requires a broad knowledge of industries and all aspects of starting and
managing a small business. In her role Ute advises business owners in all
questions concerning incorporating, business model, financials, marketing and
organizational structures. Her responsibilities extended quickly into creating
and conducting workshops and developing our services into new geographical
areas. Ute has a Master's of Business Administration (MBA) with a focus on
training and business development.
