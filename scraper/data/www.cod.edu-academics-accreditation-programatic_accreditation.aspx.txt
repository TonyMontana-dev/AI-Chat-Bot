PROGRAMATIC ACCREDITATION

Toggle Sub-navigation
 * Programatic Accreditation
   [/academics/accreditation/programatic_accreditation.aspx]

In addition to Institutional Accreditation by the Higher Learning Commission and
Recognition by the Illinois Community College Board, a number of College of
DuPage career and technical programs are approved or accredited by appropriate
specialized associations or agencies.

Some of these programs and accreditations include:
