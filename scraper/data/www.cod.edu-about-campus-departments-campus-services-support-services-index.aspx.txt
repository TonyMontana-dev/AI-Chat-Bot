Support Services serves the College of DuPage campus by providing efficient and
accurate switchboard support, managing the fleet of multifunction devices and
printers, managing the college fleet vehicles and providing creative
word-processing services to faculty and staff.


SERVICES

 * Business Cards, Envelopes and Letterhead
   
   Support Services handles the creation of College business cards, envelopes
   and letterhead. Please fill out the form below and return it to Support
   Services at support@cod.edu for processing. For questions, call ext. 3500.
   
   When submitting a request, save the PDF file to your computer before filling
   out the form. Do not complete the form in the browser. Once the form is
   complete, select "Place Order" to email the form to support@cod.edu
   [support@cod.edu].

     [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/pdf/business_card_form.pdf]

 * Campus Phone Directory
   [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/support-services/phone_directory.aspx]
 * Centralized Fax Services
   Support Services provides a centralized fax service for incoming and outgoing
   faxes.
   
   Faxes are forwarded via email in a PDF file format upon request.
   
   The fax number in Support Services is (630) 942-4359.
   
   Fax transmittal cover sheets are available in Support Services for sending
   outgoing faxes.
 * Scanning Services
   The Support Center provides a variety of scanning services. This includes
   scanning from hard copy to file, scanning to email, scanning to PDF, etc.
   Contact Support Services' staff for additional information.
 * Special Projects
   If you are in the planning stages of a new or special project, and are unsure
   which Campus Services area to contact, call Support Services at ext. 3500. We
   expand our services on a continuing basis to meet the needs of our customers.
 * College Fleet Vehicles
   [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/support-services/fleet_vehicles.aspx]
 * Duplicating (B&W/Color)
   As a convenience Support Services will process duplicating orders of
   quantities less than 100 copies. Orders totaling 100 copies or more should be
   directed to Print Services.
   
   Logo:
   Because our logo represents us at the very highest level, it is vitally
   important. All use of the College of DuPage logo, must be reviewed and
   approved by the Marketing Department, extension 3370.
 * Smart Print
   [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/support-services/smart_print.aspx]
 * Word Processing
   Material received are typed, proofed, and returned in a format the requestor
   provides. Examples of our Word Processing services include:
   * Flyers
   * Handouts
   * Fillable Forms
   * Certificates
   * Notes
   * Tape Dictation
   * Mail Merge
   * Course Outlines
   * Syllabi
   * Manuscripts
   * Letters, Memos, etc
   * Labels, Tab Dividers
   * Scanning, Converting Documents
   * Stripping, Reformatting Documents
   * Excel, PowerPoint, Publisher
   * Compiling Survey Results
   * Tests, Quizzes, Finals, Study Guides
   * Attendance and Grade Spreadsheets
   * Formatting Tests for Blackboard, Uploading Syllabi, Downloading Grade
     Sheets
 * Switchboard Services
   The College switchboard operates from 8 a.m. through 6 p.m. Monday through
   Friday. A recorded message is available when the switchboard is closed.
   
   
   
   In case of emergency, call the Campus Police Department at (630) 942-2000.

 


NEWS

 * Campus Services Department provides amenities for NON-COD related projects to
   College of DuPage employees. Call ext. 3500 or 2299 for quote. College of
   DuPage related work takes priority over personal job requests.
 * Beginning March 1st, 2020 Campus Services will no longer accept cash or
   checks as a form of payment. Therefore, all payments to Campus Services will
   need to be processed via credit or debit card.
