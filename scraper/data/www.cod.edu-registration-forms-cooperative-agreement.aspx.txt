Complete the Cooperative Agreement Request form. In addition to the form, you
must submit a copy of your valid Illinois drivers license or state
identification reflecting your current address. If you have been accepted to a
limited enrollment program, submit a copy of your acceptance letter.

Email your documents to registration_forms@cod.edu
[registration_forms@cod.edu?subject=Cooperative%20Agreement] with the subject:
Cooperative Agreement. Requests submitted without required documents will not be
processed. Applications for Cooperative Agreement must be filed 30 calendar days
prior to the start of the term of enrollment being processed.


COOPERATIVE AGREEMENT REQUEST QUESTIONS

Contact the Office of Student Registration Services at (630) 942-2377.
