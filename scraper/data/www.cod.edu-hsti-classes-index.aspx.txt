Homeland Security Training Institute (HSTI) [https://www.cod.edu/hsti/]Classes


CLASSES

HSTI offers a variety of classes for law enforcement and citizens. New classes
are added to the following categories on a weekly basis.

HSTI is going green! Most classes will require students to bring a laptop,
tablet or phone to access course documentation as paper handouts will no longer
be provided.


HSTI CLASSES

 * Law Enforcement [/hsti/classes/classes-law-enforcement.aspx]- municipal,
   state and federal sworn law enforcement. Professionals who enforce laws,
   investigate crimes and make arrests.
 * Emergency Management Academy
   [/hsti/classes/emergency-management-academy.aspx] - Local and county elected
   and appointed officials. Emergency Planners, emergency management,
   representatives of whole community partners, and governmental and private
   sector planners.
 * Emergency Response [/hsti/classes/classes-emergency-management.aspx] - Public
   safety personnel and citizens who work to mitigate the impact of an incident
   on human life and property. Individuals who plan and respond to an unexpected
   and dangerous occurrence in an attempt to mitigate it impact on people or the
   environment. Emergency situations can range from natural to man-made
   disasters.
 * Corporate Security [/hsti/classes/classes-corporate-security.aspx] -
   Professionals that plan for, identify and effectively mitigate or manage any
   developments that may threaten the resilience and continued survival of a
   business.  Individuals who are concerned with the security, continuity and
   safety of a business and its employees.
 * Community Members [/hsti/classes/classes-community-members.aspx] - All
   individuals or members of DuPage county and the surrounding area.
 * Firearm Safety [/hsti/classes/classes-firearm-safety.aspx] -  Classes offered
   to teach interested, vetted individuals the rules and recommendations on
   handling, owning, transporting and firing a firearm in a safe and lawful
   manor to eliminate or minimize the risks caused by improper knowledge or use.
 * Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) [/hsti/classes/classes-uav.aspx] – Classes for
   individuals interested in learning or earning a FAA license to pilot an
   aircraft by remote control or onboard computers. UAV commonly known as a
   drone, is an aircraft without a human pilot aboard. UAVs are used in a wide
   range of uses ranging from search and rescue, surveillance, traffic
   monitoring, weather monitoring, firefighting, photography, travel,
   videography, agriculture and delivery services.
