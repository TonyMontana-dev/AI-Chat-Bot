Online transcript ordering is available to current and former College of DuPage
students through an online order form or written request to the Office of
Student Records.

All restrictive holds on a student’s record must be cleared before a transcript
is released. You will need a major credit card to order transcripts. There is an
$8 fee per transcript. Shipping options include electronic mail (through an
exchange network or by email), pickup and Federal Express. Additional shipping
charges apply to Federal Express orders. Rush transcripts can be requested and
processed for a $25 fee in the Office of Student Records, Monday through Friday
from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.


CURRENT STUDENTS

Order your official transcript either through the student portal at
inside.cod.edu [http://inside.cod.edu] or directly through your myACCESS account
at myaccess.cod.edu [https://myaccess.cod.edu]. Both require a secure login.


ACTIVE MILITARY AND VETERAN STUDENTS

Contact the Office of Student Records directly to order your discounted official
transcript at (630) 942-2431 or emailtranscripts@cod.edu
[transcripts@cod.edu?subject=transcript%20request].


COSMETOLOGY STUDENTS

Cosmetology students can request an additional transcript with their Cosmetology
hours by completing the cosmetology request form
[/academics/programs/cosmetology/transcript-request.aspx]. The cosmetology hours
transcript is free.


FORMER STUDENTS

Former students do not need to create a COD student login to order a transcript.
Use this link to go directly to the order form. Additional verification measures
will be used to validate your identity.



Transcript Order Form
[https://www.parchment.com/u/registration/34728/institution]




LYONS TOWNSHIP JUNIOR COLLEGE

Former students of Lyons Township Junior College can order a transcript by
written request to the Office of Student Records. Your written request must
include: 

 * Last four digits of your Social Security number 
 * Date of birth
 * Name under which the transcript is listed
 * Specification of a College of DuPage transcript, Lyons Township Junior
   College transcript or both transcripts 
 * Name and mailing address of where the transcript is to be sent 
 * Student's signature 
 * Transcript payment. There is a $5 fee per transcript. Please include payment
   with your order to ensure prompt processing.

Make checks payable to College of DuPage. Send requests and fees to:

College of DuPage
Office of Student Records
425 Fawell Blvd.
Glen Ellyn, IL 60137-6599
