It's no secret that determining your future and applying for college can be
overwhelming. At COD, we can help you every step of the way. Our traditional
students are first timers who are ready to increase their knowledge and start
the next phase of their life.


START THE ADMISSIONS PROCESS

After completing the Admissions Process, continue the Steps to Enrollment
[/admission/steps-to-enrollment/index.aspx].


MEET WITH AN ADMISSIONS REPRESENTATIVE OR ATTEND AN EVENT

Admissions Representatives at College of DuPage can answer questions about
campus life and guide you through the process of applying. Schedule an
appointment [/admission/admissions_contact/index.aspx] or register for an
Information Session [/admission/visit_cod/index.aspx].


CREATE A PROFILE AND SUBMIT THE GENERAL ADMISSIONS APPLICATION

Once you submit your application, we will create your COD Username and send it
to your personal email. An email titled “COD Student Portal Username” will
contain your COD Username and information on setting up your password and
accessing your student email account.


APPLY FOR FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE OR SCHOLARSHIPS AND EXPLORE PAYMENT PLANS

Visit COD's Financial Aid website [/costs/financial_aid/] to learn more about
the financial aid process and payment options or attend a financial aid and
scholarship event [/costs/financial_aid/workshops.aspx].
