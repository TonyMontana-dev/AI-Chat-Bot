Field Studies and Study Abroad courses combine classroom-based instruction with
field-based experience for unique learning experiences. More information and
enrollment permits, when required, are available from the Field Studies/Study
Abroad office Monday to Friday, 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.


SEARCH FOR FIELD STUDIES CLASSES

Field Studies Classes  
Fall 2023 Field Studies Classes

Search for Fall 2023 Classes
[https://selfserv.cod.edu/Student/Courses/Search?terms=2023FA&coursetypes=FS&coursetypes=SA&SearchResultsView=1]



Tuition is extra and course fees are non-refundable. Students are responsible
for withdrawing from Field Studies and Study Abroad courses through the Field
Studies/Study Abroad office. Students who do not pay their tuition bills are NOT
automatically withdrawn from these courses. Prices and itineraries are subject
to change. Refunds are not given for unused portions of a field experience.
Optional trip insurance information is available in the Field Studies/Study
Abroad office.

To browse courses open for registration, search myACCESS
[https://myaccess.cod.edu/WebAdvisor/WebAdvisor?TYPE=M&PID=CORE-WBMAIN&TOKENIDX=6134724525]
by term and choose the "Field Studies" course type, along with "undergraduate"
for academic level. To register for courses, go to inside.cod.edu
[http://inside.cod.edu] and choose the Student Planning option.
