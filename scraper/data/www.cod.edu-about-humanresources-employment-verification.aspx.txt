College of DuPage has contracted with Experian Verify to provide secure
employment and income information to credentialed verifiers including mortgage
companies, lenders and pre-employment screeners.


INSTRUCTIONS FOR VERIFIERS AND CURRENT/FORMER EMPLOYEES

Verifiers must register and provide a signed authorization to obtain any
information on employees. Employees must register to be able to view their own
information in the employee portal.

Instructions for Verifiers
 1. Visit experianverify.com [http://www.experianverify.com] to log in or
    register as a Verifier.
 2. If you are registering for the first time, please select the “Commercial
    Verifier” option and continue with our registration process. You will be
    credentialed to ensure you have a permissible purpose for accessing our
    employee’s information. If you already have an account, simply sign-in.
 3. You will need the following information to request a Verification:
    * Employee Social Security Number
    * Employee’s Full Name
    * Name of Employer
    * Signed Authorization

Instructions for Current/Former Employees
 1. Visit experianverify.com [http://www.experianverify.com] and choose “I am an
    Employee.”
 2. Enter the following:
    *  Access Code: 87C2DEDE
    * Full Social Security Number
    * Full Name
    * Date of Birth
    * Email Address
 3. Click Create and Employee Account. A password setup link will be sent to
    your email address. Click the link to create a password and activate your
    account.  Log in with your credentials.
