College of DuPage Public Services division provides training programs for
individuals interested in serving their community. These short term training
programs offer opportunities to start, change or advance one’s career in a
flexible format. COD is equipping students with the skills needed to thrive in
serving others whether in the salon chair or in the state of emergency. Learn
more about each Public Service program below or call our Registration staff at
(630) 942-2208 for personalized assistance.  

 * Aviation Management [/academics/programs/aviation/index.aspx]
 * Fire Science [/academics/programs/fire/index.aspx]
 * Law Enforcement [/slea/index.aspx]
