COD NOW

COD Now [/gateways/cod-now/images/cod-now-logo.jpg]





Stay up-to-date on the latest happenings at College of DuPage with COD NOW, the
College's free quarterly e-newsletter.
 
As the largest community college in Illinois, College of DuPage works hard to
meet the needs of its 21,000 students and key stakeholders as well as help
strengthen the community.
 
In each issue of COD NOW, hear from President Dr. Brian Caputo, obtain
information on community news, learn about our students and staff, and much
more. Check out highlights from the current issue below.
 


SIGN UP

Sign up today to receive COD NOW.


MESSAGE FROM THE PRESIDENT

Dr. Brian Caputo
[/gateways/cod-now/images/slideshow/president-caputo-slider.jpg]

The Scholars Award Program is the College’s premier merit-based scholarship
program for high school students. This year’s recipients and their families
recently came to campus for a celebration as they looked forward to the start of
the fall semester.

Read President's Message [/about/office_of_the_president/message.aspx]


NEW AVIATION PROGRAM TAKES FLIGHT

New Aviation Program Takes Flight
[/gateways/cod-now/images/slideshow/aviation-slider.jpg]

Be one of the first to take advantage of the Aviation Management program
launching this fall at COD. Follow either a flight track to obtain FAA pilot
certifications or an aviation management track leading to a variety of
ground-based careers.

Learn More about the  Aviation Management Program [/news/2023/may/aviation.aspx]


A NOTE FROM JOAN

A Note From Joan [/gateways/cod-now/images/slideshow/joan-note-slider.jpg]

Every student should feel prepared for the first day of school. You can help
make this happen through donation drives for school supplies that support
families in our district.

Read Joan's Note [/gateways/community-engagement/note.aspx]


FOCUS ON SUCCESS: EBRA BUAH

Ebra Buah [/gateways/cod-now/images/slideshow/ebra-buah-slider.jpg]

Ebra Buah is the recipient of the Illinois Community College Trustees
Association’s 2023 Pacesetter Award. The award recognizes the accomplishments of
a recent community college graduate who has overcome life’s obstacles, focused
on humanitarian service and continued supporting education and community
colleges.

Ebra's COD Story [/news/2023/june/ebrabuah-pacesetter-award.aspx]
