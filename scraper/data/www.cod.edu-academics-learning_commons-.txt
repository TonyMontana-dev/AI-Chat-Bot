LEARNING COMMONS

The Learning Commons offers COD students free academic support through
course-based tutoring, math, writing, reading and speech assistance, placement
test review, general education development (GED), English as a Second Language
(ESL) support and help navigating myACCESS and Blackboard.

Students can receive tutoring support in-person and online at the Learning
Commons located in the Student Resource Center (SRC) 2102 on the Glen Ellyn
campus. Testing and support services are also available at the COD off-campus
Learning Commons in Addison [/about/visitor-information/centers/addison.aspx],
Carol Stream [/about/visitor-information/centers/carolstream.aspx], Naperville
[/about/visitor-information/centers/naperville.aspx] and Westmont,
[/about/visitor-information/centers/westmont.aspx] along with computers,
printing, Wi-Fi, college library website assistance and study space. Hours vary
by service and location. [/academics/learning_commons/hours.aspx]

Services are free for currently enrolled students. Learning Commons policies and
procedures [/academics/learning_commons/policies.aspx] must be followed by
students when working with tutors, coaches and staff.

[/academics/learning_commons/writing/index.aspx]


PLACEMENT TESTING

Placement testing is offered in the Testing Center
[/academics/testing/index.aspx] located on the Glen Ellyn main campus and at all
of the COD Centers [/about/visitor-information/centers/index.aspx]. Testing
hours [/academics/testing/hours.aspx] vary by location. Free placement testing
information sessions
[/academics/learning_commons/resources/placement-test-review.aspx] are also
available to help COD students prepare for testing.


WORKSHOPS

Learning Commons workshops are open to all COD students and provide strategies
for study skills, time management, as well as tips for maintaining a positive
attitude throughout the semester. Students can also take workshops
[/academics/learning_commons/workshops/index.aspx] in geometry and math, along
with writing, reading and speech assistance. For updates on workshops and
events, follow the Learning Commons on:

 * Facebook [https://www.facebook.com/CODLearningCommons/]
 * Twitter [https://twitter.com/codlearningcmns]
 * Instagram [https://www.instagram.com/codlearningcommons/]
