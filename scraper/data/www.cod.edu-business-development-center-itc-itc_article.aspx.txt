EXPORT WORKING CAPITAL

Looking for export working capital? These government-backed programs are some
good options.

The logical first step to take when seeking to finance your export sales is to
approach the local commercial bank that you have established a relationship
with. However, having export-related collateral can create a bit of a challenge
as some commercial lenders may be hesitant to extend export finance when the
loan application ties in with export-related collateral. Read More
[/business-development-center/articles/export-working-capital.aspx]


HARMONIZED SYSTEM 2022 CHANGES

The Harmonized Commodity Description and Coding System, known as the Harmonized
System (HS), is a system developed to classify goods in shipments entering WCO
member countries. The classification system assigns accurate duty rates on all
imports and allows the trade flow to be tracked. Read More
[/business-development-center/articles/harmonized-system.aspx]


ILLINOIS’ STATE TRADE AND EXPORT PROMOTION (ISTEP) PROGRAM UPDATES

If you are an Illinois exporter and have not heard of Illinois State Trade and
Export Promotion (ISTEP) grant, you are missing out! To most active ITC clients,
ISTEP has been one of the most utilized financial resources that provide
Illinois’ small businesses with both financial and technical assistance to
increase their exports. Read More
[/business-development-center/articles/istep-updates.aspx]


TRAVEL ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Travel Internationally Post-COVID? Find Out About Entry Requirements Before You
Start Packing.

COVID had put international travel to a halt. In the past 20 months, we all
witnessed that many tradeshows/conferences had either been canceled or postponed
aiming at preventing the transmission of the disease. Earlier this month, the
U.S lifted the travel ban for fully vaccinated international travelers, after an
almost 600-day restriction. The same thing is happening around the world- we are
seeing more and more countries easing the entry policy, however, it is
noteworthy that the entry requirements are varied by country. Read More
[/business-development-center/articles/travel-post-covid.aspx]
