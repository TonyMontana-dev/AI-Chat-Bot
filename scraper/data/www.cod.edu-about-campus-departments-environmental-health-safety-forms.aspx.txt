GENERAL SAFETY FORMS

All forms must be submitted within 24-48 hours.

 

Mgr/Supervisor/Instructor Report of Incident Fillable Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/supervisor_report_of_incident.pdf]

NOTE: download the form to your computer then open and complete the form
directly in Adobe Acrobat.

 * To be completed for every reported incident by Managers, Supervisors,
   Instructors.
 * Employee/Student must advise person in authority of reporting
   incident/accident.
 * Complete for Worker’s Compensation claims.
 * Report to EH&S

 

General Liability Fillable Form (Student/Guest of COD)
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/general_liability.pdf]

NOTE: download the form to your computer then open and complete the form
directly in Adobe Acrobat.

 * To be completed for non-work related injury or property damage on campus.
 * To be completed by COD Employee/Staff, Manager/Supervisor, Instructor,
   Student or guest of COD
 * Report to EH&S

 

Illinois Injury Form 45 Employer's First Report of Injury Fillable Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/illinois_form_45_fillable.pdf]

NOTE: download the form to your computer then open and complete the form
directly in Adobe Acrobat.

 * To be completed for every reported incident by Managers, Supervisors.
 * Employee/Student Worker must advise person in authority of reporting
   incident/accident.
 * Complete for Worker’s Compensation claims.
 * Report to EH&S

 

Employee Incident Report Fillable Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/employee_incident_report.pdf]

NOTE: download the form to your computer then open and complete the form
directly in Adobe Acrobat.

 * To be completed for every reported incident by Employee.
 * Employee/Student Worker must advise person in authority of reporting
   incident/accident.
 * Complete for Worker’s Compensation claims.
 * Report to EH&S

 

Statement of Witness Fillable Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/statement_of_witness_form.pdf]

 * To be completed by witnesses. 

 


HEALTH FORMS

Bloodborne Pathogen Incident Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/bloodborne_pathogen_form.pdf]

 * Complete the incident form and follow the instructions for submittal.

 

Hepatitis B vaccination form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/hep_b_form.pdf]

 * To be complete by COD employees going to Edwards for Hepatitis B vaccination

 

Hepatitis B vaccination waiver form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/hep_b_options_form.pdf]

 * To be completed by COD employees who elect to receive or decline hepatitis B
   Vaccine due to exposure to blood or other potentially infectious materials on
   the job

 


AUTO INSURANCE CLAIMS

Automobile Liability Loss Fillable Form
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/auto_liability_loss_form.pdf]

 * To be completed for damages involving College of DuPage Automobiles

 

College of DuPage Automobile Insurance Card
[/about/campus-departments/environmental-health-safety/pdf/auto_ins_card.pdf]

 * Carry in glove compartment of all College of DuPage owned vehicles
 * Use IL Insurance ID Card off campus incidents
 * Call College of DuPage Police for on campus incidents
