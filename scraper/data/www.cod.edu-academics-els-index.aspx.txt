ENGLISH FOR NON-NATIVE SPEAKERS

Toggle Sub-navigation
 * Cursos de inglés para hablantes nativos de otro idioma
   [/academics/els/index_spanish_language.aspx]

Learn English by taking classes in one of the following programs: 


ENGLISH LANGUAGE STUDIES (ELS) [/academics/programs/els/index.aspx]


PURPOSE

Learn English for academic and professional purposes.

 * Getting a degree
 * Updating or advancing a current profession
 * Reading, writing, listening and speaking about complex subjects


IMMIGRATION NOTE

 * Open to anyone 
 * F1 students need to contact the International Admissions Office 
   [/admission/international/index.aspx]


ADVANTAGES

 * Provides smaller class sizes
 * Targets specific skills (reading, writing, listening, speaking)
 * Prepares students to communicate with high levels of fluency
 * Prepares students for academic level classes


PREVIOUS EDUCATION

It is recommended that students have finished high school in their country or in
the U.S.


LEVELS OF ENGLISH

If students plan to study a degree or certificate at College of DuPage or need
English for professional purposes, we welcome them to enroll directly into
English Language Studies classes [/academics/programs/els/index.aspx].

Español [/academics/els/index_spanish_language.aspx]


 


ADULT ELA [/academics/continuing-education/adult-education/ela.aspx]


PURPOSE

Learn English for community participation, family engagement, naturalization,
employment or to further educational opportunities.

 * Assist with children's education
 * Better engage in community activities
 * Learn critical life skills
 * Navigate U.S. government systems
 * Prepare for high school credential
 * Obtain skills necessary for employment or career training
 * Prepare for successful transition to college


IMMIGRATION NOTE

 * Open to anyone with the exception of B, F, J and M visas.


ADVANTAGES

 * Free to eligible participants
 * Scholarships available upon exiting program
 * Offered at 25+ locations days, evenings and weekends
 * Prepares students to enter college or career training
 * Prepares students for direct employment


PREVIOUS EDUCATION

 * Students can enter with any level of education from their native country


LEVELS OF ENGLISH

 * English
 * Academic and employment preparation skills at all levels of proficiency

Español [/academics/els/index_spanish_language.aspx]

 

If students have never studied English before, they may find it helpful to start
with English Language Acquisition
[/academics/continuing-education/adult-education/ela.aspx] classes. After they
build their ELA skills, they are welcome to transition into English Language
Studies [/academics/programs/els/index.aspx] classes.
