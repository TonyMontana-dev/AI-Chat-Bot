Our goal is to provide professional, efficient processing and distribution of
mail. Using existing and emerging technology, we continue to automate the
mailing process to take advantage of postal discount incentives

To request Postal Work Order forms, call the mailroom at Ext. 2249 or print
below. 

 * Postal Work Order Form
   [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/pdf/postal_work_order.pdf]

For questions, or assistance, call ext. 2249
email files to: postalautomation@cod.edu
[postalautomation@cod.edu?subject=Postal%20Work%20Order%20Form]

Please Note: For mailings with a postal cost over $10,000, a two week notice
from the sender is required. This will allow Mail Services to take the
appropriate actions to ensure adequate funds are available in our postal account
by the requested date of mailing.

 * Delivery Change Requests
   All moves should be brought to the attention of the Mail Services Supervisor.
   This should be done in written form, by email (contia312@cod.edu
   [contia312@cod.edu]) or memo and include all pertinent information about the
   change request. Upon receipt of notification, mail will be forwarded to the
   new location.

POSTAL WORK ORDERS WITH AN AUTHORIZED BUDGET SIGNATURE ARE REQUIRED ON ALL OF
THE FOLLOWING

 * All bulk mailings
 * Any expedited services/ International pieces exceeding $10.00 per item
 * Any single mail piece or parcel exceeding $10.00


Please Note: For mailings with a postal cost over $10,000, a two week notice
from the sender is required. This will allow Mail Services to take the
appropriate actions to ensure adequate funds are available in our postal account
by the requested date of mailing.

 


NEWS

 * Campus Services Department provides amenities for NON-COD related projects to
   College of DuPage employees. Call ext. 3500 or 2299 for quote. College of
   DuPage related work takes priority over personal job requests.
 * Beginning March 1st, 2020 Campus Services will no longer accept cash or
   checks as a form of payment. Therefore, all payments to Campus Services will
   need to be processed via credit or debit card.
