Inn at Water's Edge [https://www.cod.edu/inn/]Room Rates


ROOM RATES

If you would like to check nightly rates send us an email at
innwatersedge@cod.edu [innwatersedge@cod.edu] or contact us at (630) 942-6888 to
book a reservation so we may assist you personally.

We no longer accept cash as a method of payment. Only Visa, Mastercard, Discover
and American Express are accepted.


CORPORATE PACKAGES

From event planning assistance to accommodating you and your guests, Inn at
Water’s Edge offers the business traveler comfort and convenience. Corporate
packages include a complimentary continental breakfast, complimentary Wi-Fi
throughout along with our signature personalized service during your stay.
