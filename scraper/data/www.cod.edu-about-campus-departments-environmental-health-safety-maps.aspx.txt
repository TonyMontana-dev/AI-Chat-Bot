MAP REQUESTS

To request a map, fill out the map request form below. Indicate whether your map
request is for a new map or for the replacement of an existing map. Once
submitted, you will be contacted by the EH&S Department and the areas/routes in
question may need to be reviewed in person by a member of the EH&S team, after
which map(s) will be created. This process takes approximately one week.
