ENROLLMENT CALENDAR: IMPORTANT DATES AND DEADLINES

College of DuPage is an open admission institution. Students can apply for
admission [/admission/index.aspx] at any time, as long as they have either
earned a high school diploma, a GED or they are at least 18 years of age.

The following Enrollment Calendar outlines important dates and deadlines related
to registration, financial aid, tuition payments and graduation.


FALL 2023

  Event Deadline Priority Registration Begins Wednesday, April 12 All Student
Registration Begins Wednesday, April 19 Last Day to Enroll in the Early Bird
Payment Plan ($10 down) Wednesday, May 31 Payment Due Date
[/costs/tuition/payment-plans/payment-policy.aspx] Saturday, July 1 Financial
Aid Bookstore Vouchers Begin Monday, Aug. 7 Fall Term Begins Monday, Aug. 21
Term Census Date Friday, Sept. 1 Financial Aid Bookstore Disbursements Begin
Monday, Sept. 11 Last Day to Add a Course with Instructor Permit (First 8-week)
Monday, Sept. 18 Last Day to Add a Course with Instructor Permit (First 12-week)
Monday, Oct. 2 Graduation Application Deadline Monday, Oct. 9 Last Day to Add a
Course with Instructor Permit (16-week) Monday, Oct. 16 Financial Aid Bookstore
Vouchers End Friday, Oct. 20 Last Day to Add a Course with Instructor Permit
(2nd 12-week) Monday, Oct. 30 Last Day to Add a Course with Instructor Permit
(2nd 8-week) Monday, Nov. 13 Fall Term Ends Friday, Dec. 15


SPRING 2024

  Event Deadline Priority Registration Begins Tuesday, Oct. 31 All Student
Registration Begins Tuesday, Nov. 7 Financial Aid Bookstore Vouchers Begin
Monday, Jan. 8, 2024 Spring Term Begins Monday, Jan. 22 Term Census Date Monday,
Feb. 5 Financial Aid Grants & Scholarships Disbursements Begin Monday, Feb. 12
Last Day to add a course with instructor permit (First 8-week) Monday, Feb. 19
Financial Aid Loans Disbursements Begin Tuesday, Feb. 20 Last Day to add a
course with instructor permit (First 12-week) Friday, March 8 Graduation
Application Deadline Friday, March 15 Last Day to add a course with instructor
permit (16-week) Friday, March 22 Financial Aid Bookstore Vouchers End Friday,
March 22 Last Day to add a course with instructor permit (Second 12-week)
Friday, April 5 Last Day to add a course with instructor permit (Second 8-week)
Saturday, April 16 Spring Term Ends Friday, May 17


MORE INFORMATION ON ENROLLMENT CALENDAR DATES

 * Priority Registration is for returning College of DuPage students with 30 or
   more completed COD credit hours. To view your priority registration
   credits, log into the portal at inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu] and
   locate myACCESS. Click on myACCESS for Students and from the drop-down menu
   select "Academic Profile," then select "My Profile." 
 * Census Date includes finals week for regular 16-week session (Faculty: See
   Roster Verification and Midterm Due Dates report on employee portal for all
   class census dates)
