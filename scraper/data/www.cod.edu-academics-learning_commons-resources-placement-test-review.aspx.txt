The Learning Commons offers free placement test information sessions and
workshops to help COD students prepare for testing. 


ALEKS PREP AND LEARN

After taking Test 1, use the ALEKS Prep and Learning system to review math
skills for your next test. To access ALEKS Prep and Learning, login to
inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu] and click the link in the Math Placement
Test section in the middle of the page. The Learning Commons offers one-on-one
support [/academics/learning_commons/mathematics-assistance/index.aspx]for
students reviewing math with this system as well as free workshops
[https://calendly.com/codmath/], in-person and online

The ALEKS Geometry Placement Test has a built-in review called the Prep and
Learning Module which can be accessed after taking test 1. The Learning Commons
also offers in-person geometry review workshops [https://calendly.com/codmath/]
each semester. In addition, a DIY Geometry Review
[/academics/learning_commons/mathematics-assistance/diy-reviews.aspx] is also
available. 

Students can register for a math information session directly through
calendly.com [https://calendly.com/codmath/].


KAPLAN INTERNATIONAL TOOLS FOR ENGLISH (ELA AND ELS PLACEMENT TEST)

For additional Kaplan International Tools for English preparation assistance,
schedule an appointment [https://cod.mywconline.com/] with a Writing, Reading,
Speech Assistance coach. Students are limited to eight appointments with a coach
to review for the Kaplan International Tools for English Part 1 placement test
and eight appointments for the Kaplan International Tools for English Part 2
placement test.


PART 1 PRACTICE TEST

Using the Google Chrome as your browser, copy and paste the following link to
take the Part 1 Practice Test: testflight.kaplanenglishtest.com/register/cod1234


PART 2 PRACTICE TEST

Using the Google Chrome as your browser, copy and paste the following link to
take the Part 2 Practice Test:
testflight.kaplanenglishtest.com/register/cod-ws1234
