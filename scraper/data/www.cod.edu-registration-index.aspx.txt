The Office of Student Registration Services provides a variety of enrollment
services including assistance with registering for credit classes, switching
courses, late registration, waitlist options, auditing, withdrawing from courses
and changing residency status. 

Registration staff members are available to assist students with course
registration after a student has completed the admissions application and
received assistance with course selection. Registration staff are available on
campus, by phone at (630) 942-2377 or email registration@cod.edu
[registration@cod.edu]during regular business hours. 


PLAN AHEAD FOR SPRING 2024

Are you registration ready?  Be sure to meet with a counselor or advisor to
confirm your education plan in time to register.

Classes begin Monday, Jan. 22, 2024. Check the enrollment calendar
[/student-services/important-dates.aspx] for 12-week and 8-week session dates
and learn more about tools by going to the Student Planning and myACCESS
Assistance page [/registration/myaccess-student-planning.aspx].


SPRING REGISTRATION DATES

 * Priority students (30+ COD earned credits) opens Tuesday, Oct. 31 at 8 a.m.
 * All student registration opens Tuesday, Nov. 7 at 8 a.m.

ACADEMIC TERM AND SESSION DATES

Go to the Enrollment Calendar [/student-services/important-dates.aspx] for
priority* and all student registration dates for the current and upcoming
terms. 

Go to the Academic Calendar [https://catalog.cod.edu/academic-calendar/] for
session start and end dates, last date to withdraw (without a refund) as well as
observed holidays.

*Priority registration is granted to students with 30 completed credit hours
taken at the College of DuPage. To view your priority registration credits, log
into the portal at inside.cod.edu [https://inside.cod.edu/], select myACCESS for
Students, then select Academic Profile from the drop-down menu, followed by “My
Profile.”


REGISTER BY STUDENT TYPE

New Students Current Students Non-Credit Students
New Students
 1. New to College of DuPage? You must first complete an application for
    admission [/admission/index.aspx]. Contact an Admissions Representative
    [/admission/admissions_contact/index.aspx] for more information.  
 2. Complete the Steps to Enrollment
    [/admission/steps-to-enrollment/index.aspx], which includes completing
    placement tests [/academics/testing/tests-administered/index.aspx] and
    meeting with a counselor or advisor.
 3. Schedule a virtual appointment with a Navigator
    [/student_life/resources/center-for-student-success/index.aspx] to learn
    about available resources from enrollment to completion. 
 4. When you’re ready to register, click below for more information about online
    registration. Call the Office of Student Registration Services at (630)
    942-2377 or email registration@cod.edu
    [registration@cod.edu?subject=Registering%20for%20Classes] to receive
    assistance with registering for classes. Be prepared to make your first
    payment. Learn More [/costs/tuition/payment-plans/payment-policy.aspx]

CLASS OPTIONS

 * Face-to-Face and Hybrid (HYB) classes. Hybrid classes meet mainly online with
   additional meetings occurring in person as necessary. Face-to-face meeting
   days and times for each section will vary depending on the course and
   learning needs.
 * Online (NET) or Virtual Classroom Meeting (VCM) courses. Online courses are
   taught exclusively online following instructor timelines and learning
   outcomes. Virtual Classroom Meeting courses meet exclusively online in real
   time at specified days and times using video chat software. This format is
   designed to allow students regular face-to-face contact in a virtual format.
