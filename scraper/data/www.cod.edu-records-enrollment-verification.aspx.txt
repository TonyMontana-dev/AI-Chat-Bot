Enrollment verifications serves as proof of your status as a student. Insurance
companies, loan agencies and other organizations may require a verification in
order for you to be eligible for benefits. In some cases, verification may be
necessary to prove that you did not attend College of DuPage.


ENROLLMENT STATUS

 * Full-Time Status - 12 or more credit hours per term*
 * Part-Time Status - less than 12 credit hours per term
 * Three-Quarter Status - 9 to 11 credit hours per term
 * Half-Time Status - 6 to 8 credit hours per term
 * Less Than Half-Time Status - 1-5 credit hours per term

*Dropping below full-time status may affect such items as insurance eligibility,
loan deferments and financial aid.

Summer Semester Only: Full-time status is 6 or more credit hours. Be advised
that having just 6 credits might affect your financial aid.

Military Students: The Veterans Administration determines statuses.


TYPES OF VERIFICATIONS


ENROLLMENT VERIFICATION

Request your enrollment verification online through myACCESS
[https://myaccess.cod.edu/WebAdvisor/WebAdvisor?TYPE=M&PID=CORE-WBMAIN&TOKENIDX=3416393759].
Select Enrollment Verification from the Academic Profile menu under myACCESS for
Students. There is no fee. Verification requests are completed within five
business days. During peak periods requests may take up to 10 business days.


LOAN DEFERMENT

Students who need to defer their loans should contact their lender for
specifications on requesting a loan deferment.

Loan deferments forms can be accessed through your lender’s website. Send your
signed form to Student Records at verifications@cod.edu
[verifications@cod.edu?subject=Enrollment%20Verification] or by fax or mail
which will then be forwarded to your lender. While you are in deferment, it is
your responsibility to notify your lender of any changes. 


GOOD STUDENT DISCOUNTS

Good student discount programs are generally offered through auto insurance
companies for policy discounts. Insurance companies have different and specific
regulations for granting good student discounts. Check with your auto insurance
agent/company for eligibility requirements. 

If you meet the requirements, most insurance companies will accept a transcript
as proof of your status.  Information on how to request a transcript can be
found on the Order Transcripts [/records/order-transcripts.aspx] website.


SECRETARY OF STATE

Students requesting a restricted driving permit from the Secretary of State,
must verify their class schedule at College of DuPage.

Submit the documentation provided by the Secretary of State’s office to Student
Records. You can email your documentation to verifications@cod.edu
[verifications@cod.edu] or submit by fax or mail. We will then send verification
of your schedule directly to the Secretary of State’s office.

If your schedule changes, it is your responsibility to contact Student Records.
Upon notification, Student Records will send an updated schedule directly to the
Secretary of State.


THIRD PARTY VERIFICATION

Third party verification occurs when a request is made by someone other than the
student like a potential employer or insurance company, regarding enrollment
status, degrees earned or terms of past attendance. College of DuPage has
authorized the National Student Clearinghouse to provide verifications for all
third party requestors visit degreeverify.org [http://www.degreeverify.org]for
more information.
