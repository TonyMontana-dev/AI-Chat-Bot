Carver Sprouts
[/business-development-center/images/carver-sprouts-card-image.jpg]


CARVER SPROUTS

Carver Sprouts LLC, founded in the Spring of 2021 and a client of the Illinois
SBDC, is already living its mission. The minority-owned business (operated by
Deneen and Glennis Lenoir) is committed not only to providing sustainable,
local-grown, nutrient-dense foods to underserved communities, but also to
helping close the gap of food insecurity by educating those communities on the
benefits of eating healthier.

Learn More

delivery.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/center-tool.aspx]
Ditto Foods [/business-development-center/images/ditto-foods-card.jpg]


DITTO FOODS

Ditto Foods is a minority-owned hydroponic farming company located in south
suburban Chicago with a focus on social responsibility and sustainability.

Hip-Hop Fit with Gene Hicks
[/business-development-center/images/hip-hop-card.jpg]


HIP HOP FIT WITH GENE HICKS

Hip Hop Fit with Gene Hicks is a husband/wife-owned fitness studio delivering a
unique total body workout through a blend of basic hip hop moves and
calisthenics, all set to a hot hip-hop soundtrack.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/hip-hop-fit.aspx]
letzgo [/business-development-center/images/letzgo-logo-card-image.jpg]


LETZGO

letzgo is a hyper-local digital marketing and savings platform that connects
businesses (experiences) and consumers. Businesses enhance awareness of their
brand and increase revenue while consumers save money via promotions and learn
more about the businesses.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/letzgo.aspx]
Oasis Lawn and Landscape
[/business-development-center/images/oasis-lawn-card-image.jpg]


OASIS LAWN AND LANDSCAPE

Oasis Lawn and Landscape LLC is a Winfield-based startup delivering
customer-driven landscape care and snow plowing services in DuPage County. Oasis
Lawn and Landscape started working with Doug Voegtle, a Program Specialist of
the Illinois SBDC at College of DuPage, during the pandemic in June 2020.

Learn More
[/business-development-center/business-highlights/oasis-lawn-landscape.aspx]

2020 BUSINESS HIGHLIGHTS

Ballco [/business-development-center/images/ballco-card-image.jpg]


BALLCO MANUFACTURING, INC.

Established over 30 years, Ballco Manufacturing, Inc. is a customer focused
company that machines precision CNC parts for a wide range of industries
throughout the United States. Their dedication to their customers drives them to
invest in "state of the art technologies" and "best practices".

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/ballco.aspx]
BISCO [/business-development-center/images/bisco-card-image.png]


BISCO INC.

Bisco Inc. is a quality-focused, customer-driven company, located in Schaumburg,
IL. With the mission of improving restorative dentistry in mind, Bisco has a
strong presence in US & overseas markets- the full range products for each
restorative procedure are marketed to more than 60 counties through a network of
carefully selected distributors.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/bisco.aspx]
EVOLET EVE Wine Shop [/business-development-center/images/evolet-card-image.png]


EVOLET EVE WINE SHOP

Evolet Eve Wine Shop is an experience like no other in the way to shop for
artisan wine and craft beer. The space is engineered with family in mind, so
whether or not you have your child in tow, you can get the wine shopping
experience. Go ahead, momma! Take your time and read that wine label. You
deserve it.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/evolet.aspx]
IMS Inc. [/business-development-center/images/ims-card-image.png]


INDUSTRIAL MEASUREMENT SYSTEMS, INC.

Industrial Measurement Systems, Inc (IMS Inc.) is a research and development
firm that creates measurement systems to speed product development, measure
critical material properties and improve product quality. It develops and
manufactures systems for automotive, electronics and defense industries with an
extensive background in material science, sensor technology and manufacturing
technology to produce measurement solutions that accommodate the constraints
imposed by material and manufacturing environments and to produce results that
relate to product performance.

Learn More
[/business-development-center/business-highlights/industrial-measurement-systems.aspx]
Nomic Health/Oloop [/business-development-center/images/nomic-oloop-card.jpg]


NOMIC HEALTH/OLOOP TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS

This joint venture launched in 2019 between Oloop Technology Solutions and
Saratoga Medical Center. Nomic Health, as an United States SBA 8(a) Joint
Venture, provides a full spectrum of Healthcare and Agile IT Services. Saratoga
Medical Center is a woman and minority-owned small business that has been
providing physicians, nurses, dental and ancillary personnel to the Air Force,
Army and Navy since 1984.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/nomic-health.aspx]
Prairie Gymnastics
[/business-development-center/images/prairie-gym-card-image.png]


PRAIRIE GYMNASTICS CLUB

Darion and Jillian Knight became the new owners of Prairie Gymnastics in 2018.
Prairie Gymnastics is a “neighborhood” gym, locally owned, community based and
family friendly. They offer preschool gymnastics for children 2-6 years of age,
recreational gymnastics and tumbling for children 6 and up, competitive
gymnastics, preschool open gym time, birthday parties, Parent’s Night Out, and
field trips. All of their staff members are USAG Professional members, USAG
Safety certified, background checked, dedicated to educating kids through the
sport of gymnastics, and lots of fun!

Learn More
[/business-development-center/business-highlights/prairie-gymnastics.aspx]
Sushi Ukai [/business-development-center/images/sushi-ukai-card-image.png]


SUSHI UKAI

Sushi Ukai offers a sophisticated twist on the traditional Japanese style of
casual eating. From sushi, sashimi, classic sushi rolls, bento boxes, and cooked
Japanese dishes, our menu is focused on pleasing our customers. Our elegant,
Japanese decor and friendly staff will make your visit memorable. We take great
pride in the freshness of fish. Our Executive Chef hand-selects fresh fish for
our daily sushi 7 days a week. If Dong cannot get the quality he desires or the
price he needs to make sushi affordable for you, he will not buy it. Only the
best quality at the best price guarantees to fulfill his expectations for the
customer. Miso and 'Sweet and Sour' Soups are made fresh daily.

Learn More [/business-development-center/business-highlights/sushi-ukai.aspx]
