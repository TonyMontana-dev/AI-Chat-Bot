COLLEGE OF DUPAGE SAFETY CLASSES


EXPAND YOUR KNOWLEDGE AND STAY SAFE

How do you continually protect yourself in an ever changing world? Do you know
what to do in a crisis situation or how to protect yourself from fraud or
identity theft? Whether you need a refresher or want to learn news skills, these
free courses will help achieve your goals. Conducted by College of DuPage's
Police Department, attendees will learn what to do in emergency situations where
there is uncontrollable bleeding and learn how to protect themselves from
identity theft and fraud schemes.


ACTIVE VIOLENCE PREPAREDNESS TRAINING

Monday, Oct. 2, 10 a.m. to noon 
Cost: Free

Location: SSG Robert J. Miller Homeland Security Education Center, Room 1009
                 425 Fawell Blvd., Glen Ellyn, IL 60137

Are you prepared to respond to an act of violence?  In this interactive two-hour
session you gain invaluable experience implementing the principles of
RUN-HIDE-FIGHT, enhancing your survivability and the survivability of others. 
Through the use of short instructional periods and hands-on, dynamic training,
this session leaves you feeling empowered and better prepared and increases your
situational awareness, should you ever face a scenario of active violence.


IN THIS SESSION YOU WILL:

 * Review RUN-HIDE-FIGHT.
 * Participate in a Hide Scenario, utilizing barricading techniques.
 * Participate in a Fight Scenario, utilizing threat-neutralizing tactics.
 * Participate in a Personal Decision-Making Scenario, using information known
   about a threat to implement a personalized response, using the principals and
   techniques associated with RUN-HIDE-FIGHT.

Participants are asked to sign liability waivers.


SITUATIONAL AWARENESS

Friday, Nov. 3, 10 a.m. to noon 
Cost: Free

Location: Berg Institutional Center, Room 1447
                 425 Fawell Blvd., Glen Ellyn, IL 60137

Regardless of where we are, developing an understanding and awareness of our
surroundings is extremely important.  The better we prepare ourselves, the more
well-equipped we will be when potentially dangerous situations arise. Come to
this informational session where our very own COD Police will dialogue about
strategies and techniques to help increase your situational awareness.


SAVE YOUR STASH: FRAUD PREVENTION AND AWARENESS

Friday, Nov. 17, 10 a.m. to noon
Cost: Free

Location: Berg Institutional Center, Room 1447
                 425 Fawell Blvd., Glen Ellyn, IL 60137

In this course, participants learn to mitigate fraud victimization by enhancing
awareness related to current fraud schemes.
