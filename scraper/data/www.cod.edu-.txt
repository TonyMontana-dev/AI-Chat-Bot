EXPLORE A VAST CAMPUS

Prairie, the Ecological Study Area and others).

Will counties (48 communities and 36 public and private high schools).

SUCCESS STORIES: ANAS NANJO MOHAMMED

"I’ve learned about self-responsibility and giving back to society. I feel
satisfied when I’m able to help someone and I want to continue doing so."

Learn more about Anas' story. [/about/stories/students/mohammed_anas.aspx]


SUCCESS STORIES: SAMIHA SYED

“My interest in service came from COD. There is a culture of service that deeply
inspires you because everyone at COD is a servant leader. Through my
experiences, I was able to engage with the DuPage community on a much more
personal level, for which I am grateful."

Learn more about Samiha's story. [/about/stories/students/syed.aspx]


SUCCESS STORIES: DAVID BETTINARDI

"Solving applied engineering problems is exciting, meaningful and greatly
satisfies my analytical mindset. But in the long term, I thrive on knowing that
I’m a part of something bigger that is actively working to improve the world."

Learn more about David's story. [/about/stories/students/bettinardi.aspx]


SUCCESS STORIES: AMNA RAZI
