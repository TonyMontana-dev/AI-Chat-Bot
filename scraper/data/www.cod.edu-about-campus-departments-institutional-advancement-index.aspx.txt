INSTITUTIONAL ADVANCEMENT

The Office of Institutional Advancement (IA) strives to generate grant and
philanthropic funding in support of College of DuPage’s mission, vision and
values with an emphasis on developing relationships with students, employees,
alumni, and the community. Institutional Advancement actively collaborates with
the College of DuPage Foundation [http://foundation.cod.edu] on particular
initiatives or campaigns, aligning its strategic fundraising goals to those of
the College. The teams within Institutional Advancement include Arts, Culture
and Community Engagement; Communication and Events; Grants; Alumni Relations,
Annual Giving, Advancement Services and Corporate Development.
