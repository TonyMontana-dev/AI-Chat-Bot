All English Language Acquisition (ELA) students must take a placement test and
attend a short orientation/intake session prior to registering for an Adult ELA
class. Testing dates and time vary by location. Dates and times are subject to
change.

Schedule your spring testing appointment today by contacting (630) 942-3697 or
ela@cod.edu [ela@cod.edu?subject=].

Spring term begins on Jan. 22, 2024.
