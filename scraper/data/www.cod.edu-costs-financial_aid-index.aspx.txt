FINANCIAL AID

Figuring out how to pay for college is difficult. You can save thousands of
dollars by choosing College of DuPage.

The Office of Student Financial Assistance can help you understand your
financial aid options, direct you to the steps to apply for financial aid
[/costs/financial_aid/steps-to-apply/index.aspx], inform you about deadlines and
award and process your financial aid. Free financial aid workshops
[/costs/financial_aid/workshops.aspx] are also available.

If you have additional questions about applying for financial aid, contact
financial aid. [/costs/financial_aid/contact.aspx]

TYPES OF FINANCIAL AID

Grants, work-study programs, loans and scholarships are among the financial aid
options available to students.

student in library working on classwork
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/adult-fast-track-480.jpg]


ILLINOIS MAP GRANT

The Illinois MAP Grant is available to Illinois residents who attend approved
Illinois colleges and demonstrate financial need.

[https://www.isac.org/students/during-college/types-of-financial-aid/grants/monetary-award-program/]
Two students working on a laptop in a classroom
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/inside-classroom-students-computer.jpg]


ALTERNATIVE APPLICATION FOR ILLINOIS FINANCIAL AID

Under the Retention of Illinois Students and Equity Act (RISE), undocumented
students are eligible to apply for all forms of state financial aid.

[https://www.isac.org/students/before-college/financial-aid-planning/retention-of-illinois-rise-act/]
Teacher working with students
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/cis-computer-information-class.jpg]


OTHER ILLINOIS GRANTS

The State of Illinois offers grants specifically for state residents.

[https://www.isac.org/students/during-college/types-of-financial-aid/illinois-and-federal-financial-aid-programs.html]
Two students using an ipad in a classroom
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/adult-students-inside-ipad.jpg]


FEDERAL PELL GRANT

Federal Pell Grants are awarded only to undergraduate students who demonstrate
financial need and who have not earned a bachelor's or a professional degree.

[https://studentaid.gov/understand-aid/types/grants/pell]
student drawing on paper on the wall
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/drawing-twocolumn.jpg]


FEDERAL SUPPLEMENTAL EDUCATION OPPORTUNITY GRANT

Students who demonstrate the greatest need are awarded the Federal Supplemental
Educational Opportunity Grant on a first come, first served basis.

[https://studentaid.gov/understand-aid/types/grants/fseog]
military students with flag
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/military-students-flags.jpg]


MILITARY GRANTS

Various government and non-profit organizations provide funding for college to
veterans, active duty personnel or those related to veterans or active duty
personnel.

[/student_life/resources/veterans/index.aspx]
scholarship student giving a speech
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/scholarship-student-speaking.jpg]


SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarship are awarded based on merit, need, community service, academic
program and population.

[/costs/scholarships/index.aspx]
Students in a classroom listening to a lecture
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/lecture-students-class.jpg]


FEDERAL WORK STUDY

Under the Federal Work-Study program, students with financial need can earn
income to help pay for their educational expenses by working as student aides.

[/costs/financial_aid/federal-work-study.aspx]
Student smiling in class
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/inside-students-classroom-uofi-transfer.jpg]


FEDERAL DIRECT SUBSIDIZED AND UNSUBSIDIZED LOANS

Students who qualify for Federal Direct Loans receive low interest loans that do
not have to be repaid to help cover the cost of higher education.

[/costs/financial_aid/student-loans.aspx]
teacher and student looking at classwork at a desk
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/inside-classroom-marketing-student-teacher.jpg]


FEDERAL PLUS LOAN

Students with dependent parents may apply for a federal PLUS loan to help pay
for their education.

[https://studentaid.gov/understand-aid/types/loans/plus]
two students working on a computer
[/_resources/img/three-column-card/students-on-computers.jpg]


EMERGENCY FUNDING

COD's Emergency Funding Program helps students secure immediate financial
support when urgent or emergency situations threaten their education.

[/costs/financial_aid/emergency-funding.aspx]
