TURN AROUND TIME

Turnaround times vary depending on the scope of the project and workload.

 * Copy Center: 2 Business Days
 * Digital Color: 3-5 Business Days
 * Off-Set Print: 3-5 Business Days
 * Signage: 3-5 Business Days
 * Business Cards: 15 Business Days
 * Word-processing: 2 Business Days

NEWS

 * Campus Services Department provides amenities for NON-COD related projects to
   College of DuPage employees. Call ext. 3500 or 2299 for quote. College of
   DuPage related work takes priority over personal job requests.
 * Beginning March 1st, 2020 Campus Services will no longer accept cash or
   checks as a form of payment. Therefore, all payments to Campus Services will
   need to be processed via credit or debit card.
