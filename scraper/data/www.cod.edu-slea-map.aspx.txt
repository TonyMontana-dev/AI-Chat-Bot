MAP AND DIRECTIONS

The Robert J. Miller Homeland Security Education Center (HEC)  at College of
DuPage is located at the intersection of Fawell Boulevard and Lambert Road in
Glen Ellyn, IL. The campus is accessible by driving south from Roosevelt Road or
north from Butterfield Road on Park Boulevard or Lambert Road.
