Your math course [https://catalog.cod.edu/courses-a-z/math/] choice will depend
on your math background and educational plan. You are strongly encouraged to
meet with a mathematics faculty member or a counselor before enrolling. Do not
choose your math course based on your score alone.


ALEKS SCORE - COD MATH COURSE

You will not be permitted to enroll in any courses above your score range, but
may enroll in courses below your score range.
