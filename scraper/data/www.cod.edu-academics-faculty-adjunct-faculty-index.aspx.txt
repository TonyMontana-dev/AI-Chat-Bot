ADJUNCT FACULTY

This site contains links to information that will assist you in your role as an
Adjunct Faculty member here at College of DuPage.

College of DuPage is committed to student success and academic excellence. The
Adjunct Faculty Expectations
[/academics/faculty/adjunct-faculty/expectations.aspx] define and guide all
Adjunct Faculty members in supporting this essential component of our mission.
Be sure to visit them often for updates regarding these expectations. 


ADJUNCT FACULTY RESOURCES

Information

Let’s start with the basics. The campus is large and busy. So let’s help you get
here and find the Office of Adjunct Faculty Support

 * Directions [/about/visitor-information/maps-directions/local-map.aspx]
 * Campus Map [/about/visitor-information/maps-directions/full-campus-map.aspx]
   with parking lots

Next, let’s outline what we expect of our adjunct faculty. These expectations
outline some of the job requirements all adjunct faculty must adhere to in
addition to their teaching duties. We provide this as a way to acclimate you to
what the job entails. 

 * Adjunct Faculty Expectations
   [/academics/faculty/adjunct-faculty/expectations.aspx] 

Adjunct Resources

Information on the Adjunct Faculty Center locations, Employee Development Center
(EDC), Campus Services and much more!

 * Part Time Faculty Office – These are like your office space on campus.
   Computers, supplies, small copy jobs, and a place to put your things. BIC
   2A07 and HSC 1235
 * Employee Development Center (EDC)
   [/about/campus-departments/employee-development-center/index.aspx] – An
   on-campus resource providing training, workshops, and classes to enhance your
   work environment and enrich your experiences at COD.
 * Campus Services [/about/campus-departments/campus-services/index.aspx] – For
   large copy jobs, print services and other conveniences.
 * COD Police [/about/police_department/index.aspx] – Whether it is providing
   emergency first aid, responding to a criminal act or providing escort service
   to your vehicle or class, the Police department is always ready to help you.
 * Human Resources [/about/humanresources/index.aspx] – For your compensation
   and benefits questions. 
 * Information Technology (IT)
   [/student_life/resources/information_technology/index.aspx] – Help Desk
   information and computing information.
 * Learning Technologies [http://www.codlearningtech.org/] – provides
   Instructional Design, Blackboard support and more for all classes –
   face-to-face, hybrid and online.
 * Testing Services
   [https://inside.cod.edu/organizations/org-testc/Pages/default.aspx] - Remote
   and in-person course testing for your students.

Classroom Management

For help in the classroom, whether it be questions on the Code of Conduct for
students, Advising and Counseling, you will find all you need right here.

 * Advising Tools
   [/student_life/resources/counseling/academic-advising/advising-tools.aspx] –
   Help for you and your students to navigate the COD landscape.
 * Behavioral Intervention Team
   [/student_life/dean-of-students/behavioral_intervention/index.aspx] –
   Intervention and assistance for the more severe cases of student misconduct.
 * Counseling [/student_life/resources/counseling/index.aspx] – When students
   need assistance with life.
 * Starfish – A resource for faculty to work with Navigators to help students
   stay on track and applaud their successes. Starfish is accessible on the
   employee portal [https://inside.cod.edu/employees/Pages/default.aspx], under
   the Work Tools section (requires Single Sign-On, or SSO, login). Faculty may
   also access Starfish training by logging into the portal, accessing
   Cornerstone, and then entering Starfish Adjunct Faculty Training in the
   search field.
 * Student Code of Conduct
   [/student_life/dean-of-students/pdf/student_conduct.pdf] – This is the
   rulebook for students that you should know too.
 * Student Concern/Incident Report
   [https://cm.maxient.com/reportingform.php?CollegeofDuPage&layout_id=0] – For
   reporting violations to the Student Code of Conduct.

Teacher Resources

Information about policy and procedures, the Library, a syllabus checklist,
Academic Calendars can be found here.

 * Academic Calendars [https://catalog.cod.edu/academic-calendar/] – So you know
   when.
 * Library [https://library.cod.edu/] – A great resource for you and your
   students.
 * Learning Commons [/academics/learning_commons/index.aspx] – Students can get
   tutoring services and other class related assistance here.
