APPRENTICESHIP PROGRAM EMPLOYER INFORMATION

Employers across the country struggle to find workers with the right skills due
to the growing skills gap in the workforce. Help change that by discovering
Project Hire-Ed apprenticeships.

Project Hire-Ed Logo [/about/images/hire-edlogo.jpg]






EMPLOYER BENEFITS INCLUDE

In addition to finding valuable employees, companies will discover:

 * A pipeline to their future workforce
 * An Increase in employee retention and loyalty through apprenticeships
 * The ability to foster a diverse and inclusive culture through apprenticeships

 * The opportunity to be eligible for an Apprenticeship Education Tax Credit

College of DuPage supports registered and non-registered apprenticeship
opportunities in multiple industries.


LEARN MORE ABOUT THE ADVANTAGES OF THE PROGRAM




FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find answers to your frequently asked questions regarding the apprenticeship
program.

Where can I find apprentice candidates?
Apprentices may come from a pipeline of potential candidates through Project
Hire-Ed or from your existing workforce as incumbent workers.
How do I learn more about the apprenticeship program?

Contact Project Hire-Ed to discuss your workforce needs. Complete and submit the
employer interest form.
[/project-hire-ed/employer-information/employer-interest-form.aspx]
