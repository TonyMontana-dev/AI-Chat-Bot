CAMPUS FACILITIES

Home [https://www.cod.edu/]About COD [https://www.cod.edu/about/]Visitor
Information [https://www.cod.edu/about/visitor-information/]Campus Facilities

College of DuPage offers a leading-edge campus that is well-equipped for
teaching and interacting with the community. Whether you are walking on campus
for the first time or returning to further your education, our facilities and
high-tech laboratories can give you an edge when transferring to another college
or finding a new job.

Berg Instructional Center photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/berg-instructional-center-cardimage.jpg]


BERG INSTRUCTIONAL CENTER (BIC)

The BIC is home to a variety of student services offices, as well as
student-friendly classrooms, labs, workshops, conference areas and faculty
offices.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/bic_ssc.aspx]
Culinary & Hospitality Center photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/culinary-and-hospitality-center-cardimage.jpg]


CULINARY & HOSPITALITY CENTER (CHC)

The CHC is a LEED-certified facility that offers a boutique hotel, skills
kitchens, fine and casual dining kitchens and a chocolate room.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/chc.aspx]
Health and Science Center Photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/health-and-science-center-cardimage.jpg]


HEALTH AND SCIENCE CENTER (HSC)

The HSC provides real life experiences to health science majors through
instruction in an operating room, dental lab, imaging laboratory and simulation
hospital.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/hsc.aspx]
Homeland Security Education Center photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/homeland-security-education-center-exterior.jpg]


ROBERT J. MILLER HOMELAND SECURITY EDUCATION CENTER (HEC)

The HEC is a multi-jurisdictional training center that features a full-scale
indoor street scene, forensic and cybercrime labs, command center, smoke room
and scaling and rappelling tower.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/hec.aspx]
Homeland Security Training Center Photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/homeland-security-training-center.png]


HOMELAND SECURITY TRAINING CENTER (HTC)

The HEC houses more than a firing range – the building helps provide advanced
instruction to first responders through interactive trainings and simulations.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/htc.aspx]
McAninch Arts Center Phoot
[/_resources/img/top-level/macaninch-arts-center-cardimage.jpg]


MCANINCH ARTS CENTER (MAC)

The MAC is home to three performance spaces and hosts numerous weekly events
throughout the year.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/mac.aspx]
Natural Areas Photo [/_resources/img/top-level/prairie-cardimage.jpg]


NATURAL AREAS ON CAMPUS

The Natural Areas on campus are open to the public and consist of trails,
woodlands and seed production beds.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/natural-areas.aspx]
Physical Education Center photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/physical-education-center-cardimage.jpg]


PHYSICAL EDUCATION CENTER (PEC)

The PEC is the home base of COD athletics, the fitness center, an indoor track,
racquetball courts and swimming pool.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/pe.aspx]
Seaton Computing Center Photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/seaton-computing-center-cardimage.jpg]


SEATON COMPUTING CENTER (SCC)

The SCC houses high-tech computer-specific classrooms, as well as a variety of
networking and simulation labs.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/scc.aspx]
Student Resource Center photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/student-resource-center-cardimage.jpg]


STUDENT RESOURCE CENTER (SRC)

The SRC consists of the Library, Bookstore, Conference Center, Records, Learning
Commons, Continuing Education and more.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/src.aspx]
Student Services Center Photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/student-services-center-cardimage.jpg]


STUDENT SERVICES CENTER (SSC)

The SSC is considered "Campus Central," as it is home to all student services
including Admissions, Registration, Financial Aid, Counseling/Advising, Records
and more.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/bic_ssc.aspx]
Technical Education Center Photo
[/_resources/img/top-level/technical-education-center-cardimage.jpg]


TECHNICAL EDUCATION CENTER (TEC)

The TEC encompasses a variety of COD's technical majors, including Architecture,
Interior Design, Horticulture, Automotive and more.

Learn More [/about/visitor-information/campus-facilities/tec.aspx]
